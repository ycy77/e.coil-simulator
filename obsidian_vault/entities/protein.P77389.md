Inner membrane transport protein YdhP In the Transporter Classification Database, YdhP is an uncharacterised member of the Drug:H+ Antiporter-1 (12 Spanner) (DHA1) Family within the Major Facilitator Superfamily (MFS) . BACTH (Bacterial Adenylate Cyclase Two-Hybrid System) assays indicate that YdhP interacts with the proline:H+ symporter CPLX0-7642 "ProP"; labelled YdhP concentrates at the cell poles in a cardiolipin independent manner; deleting ydhP does not affect ProP function or localization .
