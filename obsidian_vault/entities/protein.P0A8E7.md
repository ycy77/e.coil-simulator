## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Nucleotide-binding protein (PubMed:12381839). Binds ATP, GTP and tRNA (PubMed:12381839). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12381839}. YajQ is a nucleotide binding protein . Under conditions that induce MazEF-mediated cell death, YajQ is required for survival of a subpopulation of cells . An NMR structural characterization has been performed , and domain architecture is discussed . YajQ has similarity to other bacterial proteins, but is not conserved in eukaryotes . The YajQ homolog in Pseudomonas syringae pv. phaseolicola activates the transcription of late genes in the bacteriophage Φ6 . The YajQ ortholog in the plant pathogen Xanthomonas campestris binds c-di-GMP and contributes to virulence . The yajQ mRNA is a target for cleavage by EG10857-MONOMER; cleavage within the 5' UTR leads to stabilization of the transcript . A yajQ deletion mutant is more sensitive to spectinomycin and to the DNA damaging agent nalidixic acid than wild type . Overexpression of yajQ from an ASKA plasmid leads to a more than 3-fold increase in the MIC of hydrogen peroxide .
