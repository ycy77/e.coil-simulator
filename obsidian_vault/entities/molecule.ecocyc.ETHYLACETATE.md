## Enriched Summary

ETHYLACETATE "Ethyl acetate" is one of the most important small volatile esters. It is used as a chemical solvent and is used in the synthesis of biodiesels, paints, adhesives, herbicides and resins . Its annual production volume was estimated at 3.5 million tonnes in 2015, which corresponds to a $3.7 billion global market.
