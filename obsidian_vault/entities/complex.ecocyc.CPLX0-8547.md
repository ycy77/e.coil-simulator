## Enriched Summary

YdjI is class II zinc-dependent aldolase that utilizes DIHYDROXY-ACETONE-PHOSPHATE DHAP as the ketose component and a variety of higher-order aldose monosaccharides with carboxylate or phosphate termini in the retro-aldol cleavage reaction . A crystal structure of YdjI was solved at 1.75 Å resolution, showing a homotetramer in a dimer-of-dimers configuration . The ydjLKJIHGF gene cluster is conserved among most E. coli strains as well as some other bacterial genomes. Its gene products are predicted to function as enzymes, transporters, and a regulator in a potential carbohydrate catabolic pathway . However, the best in vitro substrate for the sugar kinase G6959-MONOMER YdjH that has so far been identified, CPD-23431, is not utilized as the sole source of carbon for E. coli .
