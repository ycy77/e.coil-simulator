## Enriched Summary

CPD0-2485 (Galf) is a constituent of polysaccharides and glycoconjugates that are present on the surface of the cell wall in bacteria, filamentous fungi, trypanosomatids and nematodes, but not in yeasts or in mammals . In certain filamentous fungi, Galf is found in galactomannan, in galactomannoproteins modified with N-glycans and O-glycans, and in glycolipids .
