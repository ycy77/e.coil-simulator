## Enriched Summary

It has been shown that Cystein 78 of the DsrE protein of TAX-1049 is absolutely required for the interaction of the DsrEFH complex with DsrC. This L-cystein residue is thus believe to be the acceptor for persulfide sulfur originating from sulfur stored in the periplasm .
