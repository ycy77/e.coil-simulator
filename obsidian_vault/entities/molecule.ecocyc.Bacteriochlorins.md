## Enriched Summary

A bacteriochlorin is a large heterocyclic aromatic ring consisting, at the core, of 2 pyrroles and 2 reduced pyrrole coupled through 4 methine linkages. It is obtained from Chlorins by reduction of the C7=C8 double bond. Bacteriochlorins are still aromatic, but they are less aromatic than Porphyrins porphyrins or Chlorins chlorins.
