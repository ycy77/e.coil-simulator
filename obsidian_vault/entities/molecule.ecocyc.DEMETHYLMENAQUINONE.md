## Enriched Summary

Menaquinones and demethylmenaquinones are isoprenoid quinones of the naphthalene series, and are constituents of bacterial plasma membranes, where they play important roles in electron transfer and oxidative phosphorylation. Menaquinones and demethylmenaquinones are named MK-n or DMK-n, respectively, where the n refers to the number of prenyl units present in the side chain .
