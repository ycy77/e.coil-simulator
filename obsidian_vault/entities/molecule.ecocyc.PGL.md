## Enriched Summary

CPD-18578 Phenolphthiocerol-based glycolipids, known as PGLs, have been found in TAX-1769, TAX-1768, TAX-1765, TAX-1781, and TAX-78331 . The phenolphthiocerol moiety of PGLs is attached to mono-, tri-, or tetrasaccharide, depending on the mycobacterial species. Most sugar residues are O-methylated deoxysugars. The two secondary alcohol functions are esterified by Mycocerosic-Acids "mycocerosic acids" or Phthioceranic-Acids "phthioceranic acids", depending on the species .
