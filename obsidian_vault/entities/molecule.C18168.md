## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Diacylglycerylhomoserine; DGHS EcoCyc common name: a diacylglycerolhomoserine. A precursor of the betaine lipid Diacylglycerol-NNN-trimethylhomoserines "diacylglyceryl-N,N,N-trimethylhomoserine" (DGTS) . S-ADENOSYLMETHIONINE is the active donor of the four carbon unit of methionine leading to the synthesis of DGHS. DGTS arises from lipid linked methylation of DGHS by an AdoMet-dependent methyltransferase.
