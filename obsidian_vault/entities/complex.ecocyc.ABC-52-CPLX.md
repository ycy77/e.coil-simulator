## Enriched Summary

In the Transporter Classification Database YhdWXYZ (E. coli K-12) is an uncharacterized member of the Polar Amino Acid transporter (PAAT) family within the ABC superfamily; YhdZ is the predicted ATP binding protein,YhdY and YhdX are the predicted membrane components and YhdW is the predicted substrate binding protein of the transporter complex . In strain MG1655 yhdW is an apparent pseudogene. The putative operon yhdWXYZ is a member of the NtrC regulon . This putative transporter is implicated in glycine resistance when expression is experimentally activated by a Tn5 transposase-based system .
