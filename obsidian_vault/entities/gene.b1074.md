## Enriched Summary

flgC (gene.b1074) is a gene entity. It encodes flgC (protein.P0ABX2). Encoded protein function: Flagellar basal-body rod protein FlgC (Putative proximal rod protein) EcoCyc product frame: FLGC-FLAGELLAR-MOTOR-ROD-PROTEIN. EcoCyc synonyms: flaW. Genomic coordinates: 1131438-1131842. EcoCyc protein note: FlgC is one of four proteins that comprise the rod of the flagellar basal body. FlgC is best characterized in Salmonella Typhimurium (see ); flagella structure, assembly and motor function are considered to be widely the same in Salmonella Typhimurium and E. coli. The FlgC proteins from these two species share 94% sequence identity. Early studies in E. coli K-12 identified the flaW gene within the region I fla (flagella) genes (see ). K-12 flaW mutants lack detectable flagellar structures when examined by electron microscscopy .
