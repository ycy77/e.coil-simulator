## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized lipoprotein YdeK (ORFT) ydeK is a putative autotransporter-encoding sequence; ydeK is a pseudogene in strain MG1655 . YdeK has C-terminal similarity to Saccharomyces cerevisiae Isp42p and to Neurospora crassa MOM38, which localize to the mitochondrial outer membrane . In a random transposon mutagenesis study, ydeK was found to be required for growth under optimum (rich medium at 37°C) growth conditions but not under cold conditions (15°C) or in minimal medium . However, other studies have not found the gene to be essential for growth in rich medium. A ΔydeK mutant has aggravating genetic interactions with genes involved in mismatch repair .
