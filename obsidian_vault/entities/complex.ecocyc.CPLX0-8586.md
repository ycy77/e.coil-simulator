## Enriched Summary

UspC, UspD and UspE play nonredundant and possibly co-operative roles in a pathway involved in resistance to UV irradiation; single ΔuspC, ΔuspD and ΔuspE mutants show increased sensitivity to UV irradiation compared to the wild type . Under conditions of salt stress UspC is believed to act as a scaffolding protein for the KdpD/KdpE phosphorylation cascade. UspC interacts with the usp domain of KDPD-CPLX and stable complexes of UspC/KdpD/phosphorylated KdpE/DNA have been observed in vitro. uspC null mutants show down regulation of KdpFABC production under conditions of salt stress . The protein has been detected and mapped on a reference E. coli K-12 (strain MC4100) proteome two-dimensional gel . The uspC, uspD and uspE genes appear to be coregulated; expression is induced during stationary phase; this induction is independent of rpoS but requires ppGpp . The Usp proteins (UspC, UspD, UspE and UspA) demonstrate a similar pattern of production in response to various stress conditions . Transcription of uspC in stationary phase is increased in a pgi mutant compared to wild type .
