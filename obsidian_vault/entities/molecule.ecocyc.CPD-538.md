## Enriched Summary

CPD-538 "Serine ethanolamine phosphate" is found in the animal world in both of its stereoisomeric forms. The CPD-480 "L isomer", first isolated from muscle extracts of the river turtle (TAX-31138) , is present in and is believed to be restricted to the tissues of fish, amphibia, reptiles, and birds . its function is as yet unknown . The CPD-19125 "D isomer" was first detected in and subsequently isolated from the tissues of the earthworm , where it has been shown to be the precursor of the guanidino base, LOMBRICINE .
