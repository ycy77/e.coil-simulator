## Enriched Summary

A metallophore is a small molecule produced and secreted by microorganisms to scavenge and transport metal ions, particularly in metal-limited environments. Metallophores bind their target metal ions with high selectivity and affinity. Metallophores specific for iron, zinc, nickel, molybdenum, copper, and lanthanides have been described .
