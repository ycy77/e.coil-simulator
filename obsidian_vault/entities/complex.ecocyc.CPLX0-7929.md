## Enriched Summary

YoeB and YefM are a toxin-antitoxin pair . YefM has similarity to the Axe protein of the Txe-Axe toxin-antitoxin pair, which is encoded by a multidrug resistance episome isolated from Enterococcus faecium . The YefM protein appears to lack secondary structure, and its native conformation is proposed to be unfolded. A linear recognition element which can be recognized by YoeB was identified using peptide array technology . Regulation has been described . Transcription is induced upon biofilm formation .
