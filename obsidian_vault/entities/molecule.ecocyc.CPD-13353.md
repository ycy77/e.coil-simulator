## Enriched Summary

Oxyvinylglycines are non-proteinogenic amino acids that are produced by certain bacteria. They irreversibly inhibit enzymes that require PYRIDOXAL_PHOSPHATE as a co-factor and thus have multiple targets in bacteria, animals, and plants . One such plant target is the ethylene biosynthesis enzyme EC-4.4.1.14, which is inhibited by CPD-13353 (AVG) .
