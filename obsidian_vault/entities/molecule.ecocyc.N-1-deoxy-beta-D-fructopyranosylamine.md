## Enriched Summary

CPD-25244 "Fructosyl-amines" are formed by the non-enzymatic reaction of the aldehyde group of CPD-15374 with an amino group (the Maillard reaction), followed by a spontaneous isomerization of the sugar, referred to as an Amadori rearrangement . An analysis of the form of Amadori products at room temperature has found that they exist at approximately 61% β-pyranose, 6% α-pyranose, 15% β-furanose, 16% α-furanose, and 2% keto forms . The β-pyranose form is displayed here.
