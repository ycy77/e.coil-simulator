## Enriched Summary

Folate (or folic acid) is an inactive synthetic form of THF-GLU-N tetrahydrofolate (also known as vitamin B9). Once ingested, it is converted to the active form, which is essential for nucleotide biosynthesis and homocysteine remethylation.
