## Enriched Summary

flgF (gene.b1077) is a gene entity. It encodes flgF (protein.P75938). Encoded protein function: Flagellar basal-body rod protein FlgF (Putative proximal rod protein) EcoCyc product frame: FLGF-FLAGELLAR-MOTOR-ROD-PROTEIN. EcoCyc synonyms: flaX. Genomic coordinates: 1133802-1134557. EcoCyc protein note: FlgF is one of four proteins that comprise the rod of the flagellar basal body. FlgF is best characterized in Salmonella Typhimurium (see ); flagella structure, assembly and motor function are considered to be widely the same in Salmonella Typhimurium and E. coli. The FlgF proteins from these two species share 87% sequence identity. Early studies in E. coli K-12 identified the flaX gene within the region I fla (flagella) genes (see ). K-12 flaX mutants lack detectable flagellar structures when examined by electron microscscopy . flgF mutants ('Ec:flgFi') are non-motile .
