## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized HTH-type transcriptional regulator YdcR YdcR is a member of the GntR family of transcriptional regulators . This family is known to be associated with regulation of carbon metabolism . YdcR has an HTH motif near its N terminus and an aminotransferase domain. YdcR DNA-binding activity was probed in vivo in glucose M9 minimal medium through chromatin immunoprecipitation assays (ChIP-exo) . However, the effect of YdcR on the transcription of any gene has not yet been demonstrated . The transcription of the ydcR gene is affected by UV, cadmium, and pH . The YdcR binding landscape was globally mapped in vivo by standardized ChIP-seq and quantitatively modeled using BoltzNet, a biophysically informed neural network that infers binding energy from sequence data .
