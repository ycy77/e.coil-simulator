## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Its structure and the presence of conserved basic residues indicates that it probably binds RNA. {ECO:0000305|PubMed:12837795}. A solution structure of YbcJ has been determined. YbcJ has an S4-like RNA binding domain that is expected to bind to structured regions of RNAs. Amino acids that are predicted to be involved in RNA binding were identified . YbcJ associates weakly with the 50S subunit of the ribosome and interacts with several components of the degradosome . In a large genetic interaction screen, ybcJ showed genetic interactions with a number of proteins involved in translation and ribosome biogenesis . Deletion of ybcJ appears to decrease the rate of protein synthesis and translational fidelity ; a ΔyhbY mutant shows increased levels of stop codon readthrough . This minimally characterized enzyme was found to interact with the ENOLASE-MONOMER enzyme in glycolysis in a large assessment of protein-protein interactions (PPIs) .
