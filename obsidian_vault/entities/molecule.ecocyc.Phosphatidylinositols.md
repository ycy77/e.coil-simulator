## Enriched Summary

Phosphatidylinositols are a group of compounds that include one L-PHOSPHATIDATE phosphatidyl group esterified to one of the hydroxy groups of Inositols inositol. Unlike Glycerophosphoinositols, it always contains two fatty acids.
