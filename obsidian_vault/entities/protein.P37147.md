## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Overexpression alleviates the exclusion of phage T7 in cells harboring the F plasmid. FxsA overproduction inhibits F exclusion of bacteriophage T7 . FxsA localizes to the inner membrane . A gain-of-function mutation in the fxsA promoter that causes fxsA overexpression results in resistance to coexpression of gene 10 from bacteriophage T7 with the pifA gene from the F plasmid . This gain-of-function mutation also results in resistance to coexpression of gene 1.2 from bacteriophage T7 with the pifA gene from the F plasmid . An fxsA null mutant is viable . Fxs: "F exclusion of bacteriophage T7" . Regulation has been described .
