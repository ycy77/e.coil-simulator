## Enriched Summary

The tetracyclines are a subclass of polyketides that have an octahydrotetracene-2-carboxamide skeleton, substituted with hydroxy and other groups. Many of them are potent antibiotics. The compound CPD0-1414 is produced naturally in small amount by TAX-1894, whose major tetracycline product is CPD-19258, the first tetracycline compound to be discovered . Upon addition of bromide (which inhibits the chlorination) to the culture broth, the organism produces up to 99% CPD0-1414 .
