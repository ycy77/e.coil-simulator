## Enriched Summary

A pyridoxamine phosphate transaminase activity was found in crude extracts of E. coli. The amine group donor/acceptor compounds were not identified . It was later found that D-alanine inactivates GLYOHMETRANS-CPLX (GlyA) by reacting with the pyridoxal phosphate prosthetic group to form pyridoxamine phosphate . ASPAMINOTRANS-DIMER "Aspartate aminotransferase" and ADCLY-CPLX can catalyze the transamination reaction. The identity of the enzyme identified by remains unclear.
