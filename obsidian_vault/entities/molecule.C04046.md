D-Glucosyl-12-diacyl-glycerols "1,2-diacyl-3-O-(β-D-glucopyranosyl)-sn-glycerol" (monogalactosyldiacylglycerol, MGlcDG) is an intermediate in the biosynthesis of galactolipids, which are essential for the activity of photosynthetic membranes. However, it also participates on its own in stress acclimation of cyanobacteria. The compound was shown to accumulate during heat stress , is involved in cold acclimation , and in phosphate stress adaptation , and its accumulation may be linked to fatty acid desaturation .
