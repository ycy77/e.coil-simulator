## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized metal-dependent hydrolase YjjV (EC 3.1.-.-) YjjV and YcfH have similarity to the TatD DNase . A tatD ycfH yjjV triple mutant does not exhibit defects in the export of Tat pathway substrates, suggesting that these genes are not required for the Sec-independent protein export system in which TatA, TatB, and TatC participate . A yjjV deletion mutant is more sensitive to kasugamycin and ampicillin than wild type .
