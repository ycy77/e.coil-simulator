## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Hexadecanal; Palmitaldehyde PALMITALDEHYDE Hexadecanal (palmitaldehyde) is a volatile long-chain aliphatic aldehyde. In mammals the molecule serves as a social chemosignal detected by the OR37B receptor. This was first identified in mice, where it acts as a social-buffering agent , and later observed in humans, where it blocks aggression in men but triggers aggression in women .
