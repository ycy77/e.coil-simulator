## Enriched Summary

CsrB binding to CsrA appears to interfere with binding of CsrA to glg mRNA, and CsrA acts to decrease glg mRNA stability by interfering with translation of the glg mRNA . The CsrB and CsrC RNAs each inhibit the activity of the CsrA protein . The CsrB RNA binds to CsrA protein, forming an RNA-protein complex with 1:18 stoichiometry, respectively . The CsrB-CsrA RNA-protein complex containing His-tagged CsrA has a molecular weight of 256 kDa .
