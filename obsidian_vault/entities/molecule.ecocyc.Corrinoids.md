## Enriched Summary

Corrinoids are a group of compounds based on the skeleton of Corrins corrin, a cyclic system containing four pyrrole rings similar to porphyrins. They often contain a cobalt atom. "Corrinoid proteins" are proteins that bind corrinoid cofactors. The most common corrinoid proteins are methyltransferases and reductive dehalogenases .
