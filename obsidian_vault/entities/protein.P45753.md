## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Required for the use of extracellular DNA as a nutrient. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16707682}. The yrfD gene product is required for utilization of DNA as the sole source of carbon and energy; yrfD is homologous to genes involved in natural competence and genetic transformation in other bacteria . YrfD is predicted to be located in the periplasm . A yrfD null mutant has a normal growth phenotype in complex media and in co-culture with the wild-type strain. A yrfD null mutant can not utilize DNA as the sole source of carbon and energy . yrfD is poorly expressed .
