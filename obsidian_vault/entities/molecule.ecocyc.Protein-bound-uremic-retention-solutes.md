## Enriched Summary

Uremic retention solutes are toxic compounds that accumulate in blood and tissues of patients with uremia (chronic renal failure). They may be free or protein-bound. The common protein-bound solutes include CPD-108 (p-cresol), CPD-16817, CPD-425, CPD-16818 (CMPF), and HOMO-CYS . Protein-bound compounds pose challenges to treatment since their removal by conventional hemodialysis is low.
