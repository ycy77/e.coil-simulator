## Enriched Summary

Pseudomonic acids are polyketide antibiotics produced by ORG-6360 . The antibiotic mupirocin is a mixture of four pseudomonic acids: CPD0-2581 (about 90%), CPD-21448 (about 8%), CPD-18977 (Ile]"| and ultimately protein synthesis. The main ingredient of mupirocin, CPD0-2581, consists of a C17 polyketide-derived substructure (CPD-21453) esterified by CPD-21101. For the biosynthesis of pseudomonic acids, see PWY-8012.
