yohP (gene.b4679) is a gene entity. It encodes yohP (protein.C1P609). Encoded protein function: Uncharacterized membrane protein YohP EcoCyc product frame: MONOMER0-2879. Genomic coordinates: 2228982-2229065. EcoCyc protein note: The yohP open reading frame was predicted based on the presence of a ribosome binding site in an intergenic region. YohP contains a predicted transmembrane segment, and the protein can be found in the membrane fraction . YohP is highly expressed during stationary phase . Topology assays using GFP and PhoA fusion proteins suggest that YohP may have a dual orientation within the membrane . yohP mRNA preferentially localizes to the inner membrane; membrane localization is dependent on nucleotide sequence (in particular uracil content) and secondary structure; SECE-G-Y-CPLX SecYEG and YIDC YidC are implicated as mRNA receptors at the inner membrane and mRNA binding to the membrane is translation-independent; membrane-bound yohP mRNA is translated and YohP is inserted into the membrane. This alternative strategy for membrane protein insertion may be important under stress conditions
