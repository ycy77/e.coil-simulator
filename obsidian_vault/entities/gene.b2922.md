## Enriched Summary

yggE (gene.b2922) is a gene entity. It encodes yggE (protein.P0ADS6). Encoded protein function: Uncharacterized protein YggE EcoCyc product frame: EG11244-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 3067340-3068080. EcoCyc protein note: Proteomic analysis of membrane preparations suggests that YggE forms a homo-oligomeric complex . The C-terminus of YggE is in the periplasm and thus YggE may be a periplasmic protein that associates with the inner membrane . Expression of yggE is upregulated in a superoxide dismutase-deficient strain that is exposed to oxidative stress and by UVA irradiation and elevated temperature . In microarray experiments, yggE expression was found to be σS-dependent , upregulated by AI-2 quorum signaling , and belonging to the Rcs regulon . Overexpression of yggE from a plasmid increases the maximum specific growth rate in paraquat-containing medium and decreases ROS (reactive oxygen species) levels . A yggE mutant shows increased levels of intracellular ROS and reduced tolerance to oxidative stress generated by exposure to potassium tellurite . In a large genetic interaction screen, yggE showed genetic interactions with a number of proteins involved in translation and ribosome biogenesis; a ΔyggE mutant shows increased levels of stop codon readthrough .
