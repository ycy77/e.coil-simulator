## Enriched Summary

Phosphodiesterase activity of YfcE was discovered in a high-throughput screen of purified proteins. The enzyme requires Mn2+ for activity . A crystal structure of YfcE has been solved at 2.25 Å resolution. The enzyme is tetrameric and contains two zinc ions per active site. The physiological substrate has not yet been identified . A Cys74His mutation converts YfcE to an active 2',3'-cNMP phosphodiesterase that specifically produces 3'-NMP .
