## Enriched Summary

This compound class includes peptidoglycan molecules that have been released from their site of synthesis at the inner membrane by the action peptidoglycan lytic endotransglycosylases. Peptidoglycans consists of a glycan chain of alternating β1,4-linked N-acetylglucosamine (GlcNAc) and N-acetylmuramate (MurNAc) residues with peptide stems of varying length, some of which involved in cross-links with other glycan strands. In the mature released molecues the reducing end of the glycan strand is terminated by 1,6-anhydroMurNAc. The non-reducing end is terminated with a GlcNAc residue .
