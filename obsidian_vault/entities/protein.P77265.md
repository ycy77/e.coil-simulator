## Enriched Summary

Multidrug resistance-like ATP-binding protein MdlA (EC 7.6.2.2) In the Transporter Classification Database MdlAB is annotated as a putative multidrug resistance-like ABC exporter . MdlA and MdlB each contain a TMD-ABC domain . MdlA contains 6 putative transmembrane regions and a single nucleotide-binding domain; overexpression of mdlA (cloned from E. coli W3104) in the drug supersensitive strain KAM3 did not alter its resistance phenotype against a large number of different drug and toxic compounds . MdlA is a member of the Rob regulon - an mdlA-lacZ transcriptional fusion (generated by MudJ) shows Rob-induced expression . mdlA is one of 35 efflux-encoding genes that have been deleted or inactivated in 'Efflux KnockOut-35' (EKO-35) - a K-12 BW25133-derived strain that lacks 35 efflux pumps and can be used as a platform to study their function . mdl: multidrug resistance-like
