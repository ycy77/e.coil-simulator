## Enriched Summary

CPD-17408 α-Parinarate is a conjugated polyunsaturated fatty acid with 18 carbon atoms and 4 conjugated double bonds. It occurs naturally in the seeds of the makita tree (ORG-6271), which is found in Fiji and other Pacific islands. Makita seeds contain about 46% of CPD-17408, and 34% of another conjugated fatty acid, CPD-8231. CPD-17408 α-Parinarate is also found in the seed oil of TAX-63779, where it makes about 30% of the seed oil. Because of its fluorescent properties, α-parinarate is commonly used as a molecular probe in the study of biomembranes. Like other fatty acids, CPD-17408 is rarely found in the cell in its free form.
