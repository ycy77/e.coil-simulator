## Enriched Summary

DksA and ppGpp destabilize promoter complexes and decrease the activity of RNAP holoenzymes containing the alternative σ factors RpoE, RpoS, FliA, and RpoH . Effects on unknown factors or solution conditions such as pH, salt, and template conformation can allow DksA to activate specific promoters in the absence of ppGpp . A novel role for DksA and ppGpp in contributing to the recovery from glucose phosphate stress was determined .
