## Enriched Summary

Sulfated brassinosteroids, including brassinolide-sulfate, can be produced in vitro, but the single sulfate group present on these compounds may be added at one of several different positions , represented by "R" groups on this compound. Brassinosteroid sulfates have not been detected in vivo in plants to date, but, there is some indirect evidence for their existence .
