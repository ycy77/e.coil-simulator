## Enriched Summary

The cucurbitacins are a class of bitter-tasting oxygenated tetracyclic triterpenes produced by members of the TAX-3650 and some other plants as a means of defense against herbivores. They are based on the CPD-26233 skeleton and often occur as glycosides. Many cucurbitacins induce a cytotoxic activity that inhibits cancer cell proliferation, actin polymerization, capillary permeability, and anti-inflammatory activity .
