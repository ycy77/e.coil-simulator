QseG activates the PWY0-1503 (also known as QseEF) which regulates transcription of rpoE - it is one of the multiple systems which regulate transcription of rpoE in response to a variety of stress conditions . QseG is present in the cell envelope of E. coli K-12; levels of the TKE1-RNA are significantly reduced in a ΔqseG strain and QseG is required for efficient transcription from the σ54-dependent promoter of glmY; it has no role in transcription from the σ70-dependent promoter of glmY .
