yajR (gene.b0427) is a gene entity. It encodes yajR (protein.P77726). Encoded protein function: Inner membrane transport protein YajR EcoCyc product frame: B0427-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 445302-446666. EcoCyc protein note: The YajR protein is an uncharacterised member of the Sugar Porter (SP) Family within the Major Facilitator Superfamily (MFS) of transporters . The crystal structure of YajR in an outward facing conformation has been resolved. The 12 transmembrane regions form an N-terminal domain (residues 3-184) and a C-terminal domain (215 - 386) connected by a 30 residue helical linker. YajR also contains a small 65 amino acid (residues 389 - 454) cytoplasmic domain which contains a ferredoxin-like fold. The N and C termini are located in the cytoplasm and the structure contains a central cavity which is open to the periplasm and closed to the cytoplasm . The signature motif A, which is conserved in MFS transporters, is located in the loop connecting TM 2 and 3 .
