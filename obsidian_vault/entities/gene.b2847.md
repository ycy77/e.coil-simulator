Upstream of the yqeI gene, a sequence was found that could work as a transcriptional promoter, and the transcription of the yqeI gene is affected by conditions that lead to biofilm formation and UV exposure . yqeI is located within a remnant of an ETT2 (type III secretion system) pathogenicity island that is fully present in pathogenic strains such as E. coli O157:H7 and at least partially present in most sequenced E. coli and Shigella strains .
