## Enriched Summary

ygdG (gene.b2798) is a gene entity. It encodes ygdG (protein.P38506). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: Has flap endonuclease activity (PubMed:23821668), but does not seem to have exonuclease activity (PubMed:17567612, PubMed:19000038). During DNA replication, flap endonucleases cleave the 5'-overhanging flap structure that is generated by displacement synthesis when DNA polymerase encounters the 5'-end of a downstream Okazaki fragment. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17567612, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19000038, ECO:0000269|PubMed:23821668}. EcoCyc product frame: EG12372-MONOMER. EcoCyc synonyms: xni, exo. Genomic coordinates: 2931055-2931810. EcoCyc protein note: YgdG (ExoIX) was recently reported to cleave a double-flap DNA substrate , contrary to an earlier report . YdgG was previously identified as a 3'-5' exonuclease named exonuclease IX (ExoIX) with an associated 3'-phosphodiesterase activity specific for apurinic and apyrimidinic sites . However, despite these reported activities, deletion of ydgG had no effect on DNA mismatch repair, UV resistance, or homologous recombination . Further purification showed that the exonuclease activity actually belongs to copurifying EG11073-MONOMER, and completely pure YdgG has no detectable exonuclease activity...
