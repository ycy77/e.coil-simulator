## Enriched Summary

Maltooligosaccharides are glucooligosaccharides derived from D-glucose monomers linked via α-1,4 bonds as in MALTOSE. More specifically, the term is applied to linear oligosaccharides composed of more than two and less than ten units of glucose. The corresponding α-1,6 glucooligosaccharides are called Isomaltooligosaccharides isomaltooligosaccharides.
