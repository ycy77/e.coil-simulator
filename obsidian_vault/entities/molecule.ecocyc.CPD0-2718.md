## Enriched Summary

This compound represents a membrane attached peptidoglycan polymer (meso-diaminopimelate containing). It contains a D,D cross link (also called a 4-3 cross link) formed between the carboxyl group of D-alanine in position 4 of a peptide stem on one glycan chain and the amino group at the D-center of meso-DAP from a separate glycan chain.
