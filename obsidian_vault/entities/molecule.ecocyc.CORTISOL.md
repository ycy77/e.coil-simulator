## Enriched Summary

Cortisol is a glucocorticoid steroid hormone, produced by the zona fasciculata of the adrenal cortex. It is the main glucocorticoid hormone in humans, and its primary functions are to increase blood sugar through gluconeogenesis; suppress the immune system; and aid in fat, protein and carbohydrate metabolism.
