## Enriched Summary

Cycloamyloses are large cyclic α-1,4 linked glucans that contain dozens to hundreds of glucose units. They are similar to Cyclodextrins cyclodextrins, but those are much smaller, typically containing 6, 7 or 8 glucose units. Cycloamyloses are formed from high molecular weight Alpha-Amyloses by the action of EC-2.4.1.25. Cycloamyloses are large cyclic α-1,4 linked glucans that contain dozens to hundreds of glucose units.
