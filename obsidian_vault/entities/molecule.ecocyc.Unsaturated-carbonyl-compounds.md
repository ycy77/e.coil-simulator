## Enriched Summary

α,β-Unsaturated carbonyl compounds are an important class of carbonyl compounds in which the carbonyl group is conjugated with an alkene. They have the general structure (O=CR)-Cα=Cβ-R. Unlike simple carbonyls, α,β-unsaturated carbonyl compounds are often attacked by nucleophiles at the β carbon.
