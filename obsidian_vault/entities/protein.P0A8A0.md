Probable transcriptional regulatory protein YebC At 20°C and in the absence of the protein EG11837-MONOMER BipA, YebC appears to regulate genes involved in biofilm formation, LPS synthesis, capsule production, and resistance to bile salts yebC insertion mutants were identified in a genetic screen for genes that are important for survival of exposure to ionizing radiation (IR). A yebC deletion mutant has a substantial decrease in IR survival . A yebC null mutant grows normally at 37°C , but is more sensitive to nalidixic acid and kasugamycin than wild type .
