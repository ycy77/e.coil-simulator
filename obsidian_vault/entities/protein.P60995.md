## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: This protein is involved in the attenuation mechanism for the control of the expression of the his operon structural genes. The HisL leader peptide controls by attenuation the transcription of the hisLGDCBHAFI operon (which encodes the enzymes of the histidine biosynthesis pathway). HisL contains seven histidine residues as its attenuation regulatory points within a 16 amino acid long peptide . Moreover, the hisL and the tRNAHis sequences have been detected to be homologs, implying that both can interact with or be the target of the same regulatory effects , which may have important effects in the operon expression.
