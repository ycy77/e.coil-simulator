## Enriched Summary

ybcL (gene.b0545) is a gene entity. It encodes ybcL (protein.P77368). Encoded protein function: UPF0098 protein YbcL EcoCyc product frame: G6301-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 570893-571444. EcoCyc protein note: YbcL from uropathogenic E. coli (strain UTI89) suppresses acute neutrophil migration which is associated with pathogenicity . There are 6 amino acid differences between YbcL from E. coli K-12 MG1655 and strain UTI89 - one of which (V78T) is non-conservative. Episomal expression of YbcL UTI89(T78V) in an E. coli UTI189 ybcL deleted strain results in loss of function (ie. lack of suppression of neutrophils in a transepithelial migration assay) whereas expression of YbcLMG1655(V78T) in the same background results in a gain-of-function phenotype . Expression of YbcLUTI89 or YbcLMG1655(V78T) in E. coli K-12 MG1655 results in suppression of neutrophil migration levels similar to those seen in E. coli UTI89 . YbcL from uropathogenic E. coli UTI89 is secreted from the bacterial periplasm during infection . YbcL has sequence and structural similarity to rat and human RKIP (Raf kinase inhibitor protein), which modulates signal transduction pathways . A crystal structure of the protein has been solved at 1.8 Å resolution . YbcL is periplasmic .
