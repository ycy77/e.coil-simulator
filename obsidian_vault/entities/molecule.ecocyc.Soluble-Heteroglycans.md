## Enriched Summary

This class of soluble heteroglycans has been identified in Arabidopsis plants and is involved in the cytosolic metabolism of maltose released from the chloroplast during starch degradation . Various subfractions of this broad class of water-soluble heteoglycans have been identified in various plant species including Arabidopsis and Pisum sativum (peas) and their sugar composition, sugar linkage profile and degree of branching have been characterized in various organs at different times of day .
