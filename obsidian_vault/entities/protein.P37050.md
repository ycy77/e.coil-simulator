## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Part of the yadCKLM-htrE-yadVN fimbrial operon. Could contribute to adhesion to various surfaces in specific environmental niches. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20345943}. yadNVhtrEyadMLKC is a putative chaperone-usher fimbrial operon in E.coli K-12 . The operon is cryptic under normal laboratory conditions but when constitutive expression is induced in a strain that lacks the type 1 fimbrial complex the yad operon promotes biofilm formation in minimal media on a variety of abiotic surfaces and produces surface fimbrial structures that can be observed microscopically . Constitutive expression of the yad operon results in increased adhesion of cells to xylose; constitutive expression of the yad operon results in increased adherence to intestinal epithelial cells and can modulate the inflammatory reponse of host cells . A strain producing Yad fimbriae outcompetes both the wild type and a Δyad strain for corn rhizosphere colonisation YadN is the most abundant protein in Yad fimbrial extractions; YadN is an important component of the fimbrial shaft; deletion of yadN in a strain that produces Yad fimbriae results in disappearance of these structures and abolishes Yad-mediated adhesion to abiotic surfaces . Expression of the yad operon is negatively regulated by PD00288 "H-NS"...
