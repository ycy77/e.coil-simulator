## Enriched Summary

The Carbohydrates carbohydrate definition includes Monosaccharides monosaccharides, Disaccharides disaccharides, Oligosaccharides oligosaccharides and Polysaccharides polysaccharides as well as substances derived from monosaccharides by reduction of the carbonyl group (Sugar-alcohols alditols), by oxidation of one or more terminal groups to Carboxylates "carboxylic acids", or by replacement of one or more hydroxy group(s) by a hydrogen atom, an amino group, a sulfhydryl group or similar heteroatomic groups. It also includes oligo- and polysaccharides consisting of such modified monosaccharides, as well as derivatives of these compounds. Even though the carbohydrate definition includes derivatives, we list substances generated by further modifications (e.g., acetylation, phosphorylation) under this class as "a carbohydrate derivative" for clarification purposes.
