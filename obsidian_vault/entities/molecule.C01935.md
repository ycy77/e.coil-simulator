KEGG compound: Maltodextrin; Maltodextrin(n); Maltodextrin(n-2); n+2 Maltodextrin; Linear maltodextrin; Maltooligosaccharide EcoCyc common name: a maltodextrin. Maltodextrins are linear polymers of glucose molecules joined by an O-glycosidic (α-1->4) bond. The term is usally used to describe a mixture of chains that vary from 3-17 glucose units long. Maltodextrin is commonly used as a food additive, and is commercially produced from corn and wheat Starch by partial hydrolysis.
