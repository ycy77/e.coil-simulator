## Enriched Summary

Spectroscopic studies of the biosynthesis of CPD-12379 showed that it takes place in three chromophoric phases. The first phase corresponds to the formation of a red pigment (λmax 475 nm), L-DOPACHROME. The second phase corresponds to a purple intermediate, which was designated CPD-12378, with a broad absorption maximum at 540 nm, and the third phase is characterized by a general absorption due to CPD-12379 . Chemical analysis of CPD-12378 suggests that it is composed of different oligomers (mostly dimers and trimers) of DIHYDROXYINDOLE (DHI) and 56-DIHYDROXYINDOLE-2-CARBOXYLATE linked by 2-4' and 2-7' linkages .
