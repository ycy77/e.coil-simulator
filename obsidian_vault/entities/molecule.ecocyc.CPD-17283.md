## Enriched Summary

CPD-8120 "Di-homo-γ-linolenate" (dihomo-γ-linolenic acid) is a 20-carbon ω-6 polyunsaturated fatty acid with a 20-carbon chain and three cis double bonds at positions 8, 11 and 14. Di-homo-γ-linolenate is formed by elongation of CPD-8117, and is usually converted rapidly to other compounds. However, it has been detected in small amounts as a component of cellular lipids in fungi , algae , protozoa , and animals . Like other fatty acids, CPD-8120 is rarely found in its free form. It is usually found either as CPD-14407 or CPD-17283 "incorporated into a lipid".
