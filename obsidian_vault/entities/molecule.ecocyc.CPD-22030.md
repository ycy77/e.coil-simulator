## Enriched Summary

CPD-22030 Muscimol is one of the principal psychoactive constituents of TAX-41956 and related species of mushrooms. It is a potent, selective agonist for the GABAA receptors with exceptionally high affinity to GABAA-delta receptors . GABAA receptors are widely distributed in the brain, and so when muscimol is administered, it alters neuronal activity in multiple regions including the cerebral cortex, hippocampus, and cerebellum, resulting in sedative-hypnotic, depressant and hallucinogenic psychoactivity. CPD-22030 Muscimol is produced by decarboxylation of CPD-23410. CPD-22030 Muscimol is one of the principal psychoactive constituents of TAX-41956 and related species of mushrooms.
