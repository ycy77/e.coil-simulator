## Enriched Summary

Ellagic acid is an organic heterotetracyclic compound that is formed by the dimerization of CPD-183 by oxidative aromatic coupling that involves intramolecular lactonization of both carboxylic acid groups of the resulting biaryl. It is found in many fruits and vegetables, including raspberries, strawberries, cranberries, and pomegranates, mostly in a bound form as part of different Ellagitannins ellagitannins.
