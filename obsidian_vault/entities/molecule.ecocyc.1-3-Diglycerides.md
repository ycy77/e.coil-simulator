## Enriched Summary

The name glyceride describes any ester of GLYCEROL with Fatty-Acids "fatty acids". Depending on the number of fatty acids, glycerides are classified as Monoacylglycerols monoglycerides, Diacylglycerides diglycerides and Triacylglycerols triglycerides. Diglycerides are classified further into 1-2-Diglycerides "1,2-diglycerides" and 1-3-Diglycerides "1,3-diglycerides".
