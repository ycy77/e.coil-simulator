## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized protein YihR YihR may be a sulfoquinovose mutarotase. The gene cluster encoding enzymes of the sulfoquinovose degradation pathway is conserved. The YihR homolog of Herbaspirillum seropedicaea was shown to have sulfoquinovose mutarotase activity . Note, however, that the best BLAST hit of the H. seropedicaea protein in the E. coli genome is G7338-MONOMER YphB rather than YihR. YihR appears to be involved in biofilm formation. A mutant with a deletion of yihR shows a significant decrease in biofilm formation . Expression of yihR increases 11-fold upon deletion of tqsA (which increases biofilm formation) , and is induced by growth on sulfoquinovose .
