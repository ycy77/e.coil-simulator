Cannot synthesize Gsp, glutathione (GSH), or GSH intermediates, from GSH and spermidine, cysteine and glutamate, gamma-glutamylcysteine and spermidine, and gamma-glutamylcysteine and glycine. Does not bind to Gsp. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23097746}. EcoCyc product frame: EG11812-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 4413028-4414191.
