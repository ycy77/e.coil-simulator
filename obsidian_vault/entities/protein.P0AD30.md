Probable membrane transporter protein YfcA YfcA is predicted to be an inner membrane protein with seven transmembrane domains; experimental topology analysis suggests the C terminus is in the periplasm . yfcA shows differential codon adaptation, resulting in differential translation efficiency signatures, in thermophilic microbes and is predicted to play a role in the heat shock response. A yfcA deletion mutant is more sensitive than wild-type specifically to heat shock, but not other stresses . In the Transporter Classification Database YfcA is an uncharacterized member of the 4-Toluene Sulfonate Uptake Permease (TSUP) family.
