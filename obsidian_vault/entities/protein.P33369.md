## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Could be involved in resistance to puromycin, acriflavine and tetraphenylarsonium chloride. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11257026}. Sequence analysis suggests mdtQ encodes the outer membrane factor of a multidrug efflux system; deletion of mdtQ from strain W3110 results in increased susceptibility to acriflavin, puromycin, and tetraphenylarsonium chloride . Note: EcoGene indicates that mdtQ is a pseudogene in strain MG1655 and that intact alleles are present in many E. coli strains. The mutant phenotype observed by may be due to the presence of a functional gene in the strain used by these authors. Sequence differences between W3110 and MG1655 have been documented in ; mdtQ was not one of the 8 confirmed sequence differences noted.
