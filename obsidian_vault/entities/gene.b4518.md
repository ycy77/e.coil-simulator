These two genes also show homology to gene PA2146 in TAX-208964, shown to contribute to both biofilm formation and susceptibility to a variety of antimicrobial agents . Deletion of both yciG and ymdF together exhibited a slight swimming motility defect . ymdF can substitute for PA2146 gene in P. aeruginosa ΔPA2146 mutants.
