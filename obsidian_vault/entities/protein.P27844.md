## Enriched Summary

Protein RarD RarD is predicted to be an inner membrane protein with nine transmembrane domains; topology analysis suggests the C- terminus is located in the cytoplasm . RarD structure has been modelled . rarD expression increases under anaerobiosis and decreases in an fnr deficient strain and in an arcA deficient strain . A putative ArcA binding site is located 560 bp upstream of rarD but no promoter has been identified. In the Transporter Classification Database, RarD is a member of the Chloramphenicol-Sensitivitiy Protein (RarD) Family within the Drug/Metabolite Transporter (DMT) superfamily . A variety of potential substrates have been identified using an untargeted metabolomics approach .
