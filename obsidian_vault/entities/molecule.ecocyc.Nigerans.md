## Enriched Summary

Nigeran (mycodextran) is a water insoluble D-glucan found in the hypal wall of fungi such as TAX-5061 and TAX-36656. This unbranced D-Glucan consists of alternating α-1,3 and α-1,4-glucosidic linkages.
