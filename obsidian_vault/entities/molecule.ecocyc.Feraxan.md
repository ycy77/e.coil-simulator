## Enriched Summary

Feraxan is a complex polysaccharide consisting of Arabinoxylan arabinoxylan molecules decorated by feruloyl groups that are ester-linked to the arabinose residues. The ferulate residues form cross links between adjacent arabinoxylan molecules. Feraxan is one of the major components of soluble fibre from cereals such as rice and ragi .
