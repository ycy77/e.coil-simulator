## Enriched Summary

Cysteine tryptophylquinone (CTQ), the cofactor of CPLX-8087 (QHNDH), consists of an orthoquinone-modified tryptophan side chain covalently linked to a nearby cysteine side chain, and catalyzes the oxidative deamination of primary amines . CTQ is similar to CPD-12145 (TTQ), the cofactor of CPLX-3248.
