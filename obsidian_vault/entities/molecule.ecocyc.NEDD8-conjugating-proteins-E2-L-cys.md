## Enriched Summary

The NEDD8-carrier-proteins-E2 "E2 NEDD8-conjugating enzymes" perform the second step in the neddylation process. The activity of the enzyme starts with the transfer of NEDD8 from an NEDD8-activating-proteins-E1 "[E1 NEDD8-activating enzyme]"-NEDD8 NEDD8 complex to a catalytic L-cysteine residue at the active site by transesterification. In the presence of the appropriate E3 ligases, the NEDD8 protein is then transferred to a lysine residue of specific substrates.
