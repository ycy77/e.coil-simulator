## Enriched Summary

PaoD is the private chaperone for the insertion of sulfurated molybdopterin cytosine dinucleotide cofactor (MCD) into the aromatic aldehyde oxidoreductase PaoABC . PaoD interacts with G7496-MONOMER and binds molybdenum cofactor . PaoABC expressed in the absence of PaoD is inactive and lacks molybdenum cofactor .
