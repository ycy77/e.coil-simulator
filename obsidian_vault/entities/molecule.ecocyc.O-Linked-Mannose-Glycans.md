## Enriched Summary

O-Linked mannose glycans are initiated by covalent linkage of mannose to the hydroxyl oxygen of a serine or threonine amino acid residue. O-Mannose may then be extended by the addition of other monosaccharides and functional groups to form a variety of glycan structures.
