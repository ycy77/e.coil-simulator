## Enriched Summary

Pinoresinol is a lignan, produced by by radical β-β' coupling of two CONIFERYL-ALCOHOL molecules. It is produced by plants as a defense molecule, and is a component of Lignins lignin. Pinoresinol is known to be liberated from lignin during the degradation of dead plant material by white rot fungi and to be degraded by bacteria . Pinoresinol is contained in cereals, particularly in whole-grain rye products, in olive oil,and in various members of the TAX-3318 family, from the TAX-3328, TAX-3337 and TAX-3319 genera.
