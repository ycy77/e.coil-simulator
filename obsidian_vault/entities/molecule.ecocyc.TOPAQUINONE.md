## Enriched Summary

Topaquinone (TPQ) is a ubiquitous quinone cofactor, existing in both prokaryotic and eukaryotic enzymes that include a divergent class of copper-containing amine oxidases. It is synthesized as a post-translational modification of a tyrosine residue within the polypeptide chain of the enzymes. TPQ is formed by self-processed oxidation of a specific TYR residue in the presence of copper ion and molecular oxygen .
