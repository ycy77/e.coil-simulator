## Enriched Summary

Microcins are a class of gene-encoded low-molecular-mass antibacterial peptides secreted by bacteria. Microcins produced by commensal TAX-562 strains target and eliminate enteric pathogens such as TAX-28901 by mimicking the siderophores the pathogens use for iron scavenging.
