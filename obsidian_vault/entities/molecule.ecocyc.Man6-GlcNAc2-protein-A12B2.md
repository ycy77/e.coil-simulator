## Enriched Summary

This is one of three isomers of Man6GlcNAc2 chains that are found in N glycans. The nomenclature used to identify high mannose glycans has not been standardized. Several earlier systems were insufficient to describe all possible isomers . The nomenclature used here is that proposed by Prien et al , which can represent all processing isomers. According to this nomenclature, the other two Man6GlcNAc2 isomers are named Man6GlcNAc2-protein-A123 "isomer 6A1,2,3" and Man6GlcNAc2-protein-A12B1 "isomer 6A2,3B1".
