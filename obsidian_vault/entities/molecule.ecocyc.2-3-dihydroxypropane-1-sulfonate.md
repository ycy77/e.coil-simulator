## Enriched Summary

2-3-dihydroxypropane-1-sulfonate "2,3-Dihydroxypropane-1-sulfonate" (DHPS) is a widespread intermediate in plant and algal transformations of CPD-10247 from the plant sulfolipid SULFOQUINOVOSYLDIACYLGLYCEROL . It is also recovered quantitatively during bacterial degradation of CPD-10247 , and is secreted by all diatoms .
