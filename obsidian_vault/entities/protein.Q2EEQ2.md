Large ribosomal subunit protein bL36B (50S ribosomal protein L36 2) YkgO is a paralog of RpmJ, the EG11232-MONOMER, but lacks its zinc binding motif . YkgO is the prevalent paralog present in stationary phase ribosomes . L36B functionally substitutes for L36 .
