## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Found in membrane microdomains that may be equivalent to eukaryotic membrane rafts (PubMed:20713508). FMMs are highly dynamic and increase in number as cells age. Flotillins are thought to be important factors in membrane fluidity (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O32076, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20713508}. yqiK encodes a flotillin-like protein . YqiK is a prokaryotic member of the stomatin, prohibitin, flotillin, and HflK/C (SPFH) protein superfamily ; SPFH domain proteins localize in eukaryotic lipid rafts and the function and localization of E. coli SPFH proteins, including YqiK has been investigated (see also ). YqiK is predicted to be a bitopic inner membrane protein with its C-terminus located in the cytoplasm . YqiK fluorescent fusion proteins (YqiK-GFP and Yqik-YFP) localize to discrete foci in the membrane . The YqiK fusion protein with enhanced green fluorescent protein (YqiK-EGFP) distributes throughout the cytoplasm . A ΔyqiK strain shows enhanced resistance to ampicillin and enhanced swimming behaviour .
