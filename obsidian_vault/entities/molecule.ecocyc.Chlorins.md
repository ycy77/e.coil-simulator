## Enriched Summary

A chlorin is a large heterocyclic aromatic ring consisting of 3 pyrroles and one reduced pyrrole, coupled through 4 methine linkages. Unlike Porphyrins, a chlorin is largely aromatic, but not aromatic through the entire circumference of the ring. Chlorophylls are Mg-containing chlorin derivatives.
