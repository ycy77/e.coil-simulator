## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: This protein is involved in control of the biosynthesis of isoleucine, leucine, and valine. The ilvBN operon leader peptide (IvbL) controls by attenuation the expression of the TU00032 operon, which codes for an enzyme involved in isoleucine and valine biosynthesis. The attenuator region contains both valine and leucine residues and several potential stem-loop structures, yielding negative regulation in response to valine and leucine levels. It also contains regions similar to those found in CRP-dependent promoters, yielding positive regulation in response to cyclic AMP and CRP .
