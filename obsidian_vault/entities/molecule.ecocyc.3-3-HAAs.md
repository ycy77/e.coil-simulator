## Enriched Summary

This class contains molecules that are composed of two 3-hydroxy fatty acid chains of varying length (represented here in part by R1 and R2) linked to each other through an ester bond between the β-hydroxyl group of one chain and the carboxyl group of another chain. They have been found to be constituents of rhamnolipids (see Mono-Rhamnolipids and a Di-Rhamnolipids) .
