## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Fructan; beta-D-Fructan EcoCyc common name: a fructan. Fructans are a class of highly water-soluble polysaccharides consisting of linear or branched FRU chains attached to SUCROSE by β-2,1or β-2,6-linkage. There are 3 types of fructans: 1. Inulin - linear fructans generally linked by β(2->1) glycosidic bonds 2. Levan - linear fructans generally linked by β(2->6) glycosidic bonds 3. Graminans - branched fructans linked by both β(2->1) and β(2->6) glycosidic bonds. The fructosyl units may be linked to the sucrose base unit either to the fructose moiety, or to the glucose moiety by a β 2->6 bond. In the latter case, the resulting molecule name is marked by a 6G, as in NEOKESTOSE 6G-kestotriose.
