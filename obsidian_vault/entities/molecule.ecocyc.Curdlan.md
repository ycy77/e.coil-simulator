## Enriched Summary

Curdlan is a linear high-molecular-weight 1,3-β-D-glucan exopolysaccharide polymer produced by by soil bacteria of the family Rhizobiaceae . Curdlan has numerous applications as a gelling agent in the food, construction, and pharmaceutical industries and has been approved as a food additive by the U. S.
