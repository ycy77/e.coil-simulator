## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Magnesium cation; Mg2+; Magnesium(2+); Magnesium ion EcoCyc common name: Mg2+. Many hundreds of enzymes require magnesium ions (Mg2+) for their catalytic action, including all enzymes using or synthesizing ATP and those that use other nucleotides to synthesize DNA and RNA. The interaction between magnesium ions and phosphate makes magnesium essential to basic nucleic acid chemistry, and the common ATP molecule is normally found in a chelate with a magnesium ion. Photosynthetic organisms require Mg for the biosynthesis of chlorophyll, which is essential for photosynthesis. The function of magnesium in the center of the porphyrin ring in chlorophyll is similar to that of iron in the center of the porphyrin ring in heme. Mg is the fourth-most abundant element in the human body (Ca2+ > K+ > Na+ > Mg2+).
