## Enriched Summary

CPD-23999 2-heptyl-1-hydroxy-4(1H)-quinolinone is a tautomer of CPD-7975 (HQNO), which is a respiratory toxin produced by TAX-287 that suppresses the growth of many Gram-positive bacteria . This 1H-4-oxo tautomer is presumed to prevail in aqueous solutions at pH 7 .
