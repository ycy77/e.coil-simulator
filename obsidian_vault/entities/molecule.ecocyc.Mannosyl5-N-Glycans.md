## Enriched Summary

This is one of four possible isomers of Man5GlcNAc2 chains. It is the turning point between the processing phase of high-mannose N-glycans and the synthesis phase of complex and hybrid N-glycans. It is the substrate for the first N-acetylglucosaminyltransferase that is involved in the synthesis of the latter, EC-2.4.1.101. The nomenclature used to identify high mannose glycans has not been standardized. Several earlier systems were insufficient to describe all possible isomers . The nomenclature used here is that proposed by Prien et al , which can represent all processing isomers. According to this nomenclature, the other three Man5GlcNAc2 isomers are named isomer 5A2,3, isomer 5A1,3, and isomer 5A2B2.
