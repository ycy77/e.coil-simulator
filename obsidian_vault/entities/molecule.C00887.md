## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Nitrous oxide; Dinitrogen monoxide; Dinitrogen oxide; N2O Nitrous oxide is a major greenhouse gas with important impacts on the environment. Its 100-year global warming potential is about 320 times as strong as that of carbon dioxide due to its a lifetime of approximately 120 years. The major sink for NITROUS-OXIDE is the stratospheric reaction with atomic oxygen to form NITRIC-OXIDE, which induces the destruction of stratospheric ozone. Nitrous oxide is the main naturally occurring regulator of stratospheric ozone.
