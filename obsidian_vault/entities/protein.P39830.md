Putative cation/proton antiporter YbaL The YbaL protein is an uncharacterised member of the CPA2 family of monovalent cation:proton antiporters . ybaL has supercoiling-dependent transcription, which is associated with the osmotic stress response and acts through rpoS . In experimental evolution experiments requiring adaptation of E. coli to high growth temperature (45.9°C or 42.2°C), ybaL is frequently mutated .
