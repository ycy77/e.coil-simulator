## Enriched Summary

CPD-2482 is an antibiotic substance produced by several TAX-1883 species . The compound, which is a cyclic analog of D-alanine, prevents the catalytic activities of both EC-5.1.1.1 and EC-6.3.2.4 , which are necessary for the biosynthesis of the bacterial cell wall. It is clinically used as a second-line of defense against TAX-1773, especially when the bacterium is resistant to other antituberculosis antibiotics . CPD-2482 is also a candidate for the treatment of various psychological dysfunctions, since it has been shown to be a partial agonist for the CPD-10705 receptor .
