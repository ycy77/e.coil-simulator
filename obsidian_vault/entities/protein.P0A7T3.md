## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: In addition to being a ribosomal protein, S16 also has a cation-dependent endonuclease activity. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8730873}.; FUNCTION: In-frame fusions with the ribosome maturation factor rimM suppress mutations in the latter (probably due to increased rimM expression) and are found in translationally active 70S ribosomes. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11514519}. The S16 protein is a component of the 30S subunit of the ribosome and is essential for viability . S16 interacts with the 5' domain of 16S rRNA . Binding of S16 to 16S rRNA is dependent on prior binding of S4 and S20 . Subsequent binding of S16 suppresses non-native assembly intermediates and drives a conformational switch at helix 3 of 16S rRNA, stabilizing the decoding center of the 30S subunit . Ensemble FRET experiments confirmed that S16 destabilizes the non-native S4-rRNA conformation . S16 was also shown to be a DNA-binding protein with Mg2+-Mn2+-dependent endonuclease activity . S16 preferentially binds to cruciform DNA structures; its endonuclease activity appears to be sequence-specific . S16 may bind Zn2+ . Expression of rpsP is induced 16-fold upon exposure of cells to the biocide polyhexamethylene biguanide .
