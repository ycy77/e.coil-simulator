## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: This protein is involved in control of the biosynthesis of threonine. The ThrL leader peptide controls by attenuation the expression of the thrLABC operon, which encodes four out of the five enzymes of threonine biosynthesis pathway, in response to the threonine and isoleucine levels . ThrL is a 21 amino acid long peptide, with eight threonine and four isoleucine residues as regulatory points during attenuation .
