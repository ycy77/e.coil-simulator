## Enriched Summary

3-Hydroxy-11-cis-retinal is a retinoid used as the visual pigment chromophore in some insects . When the receptor absorbs light photons the retinal undergoes a conformation change to All-trans-3-hydroxyretinal . Most of the insect visual pigments use the (3R)-3-hydroxyretinal enantiomer as the visual pigment chromophore. However, members of the higher Diptera, the TAX-480117, have been shown to synthesize and use the (3S)-3-hydroxyretinal enantiomer as chromophore . 3-Hydroxy-11-cis-retinal is a retinoid used as the visual pigment chromophore in some insects .
