## Enriched Summary

Fatty acyl-CoAs are ACYL-COA "acyl-CoAs" derived from Fatty-Acids "fatty acids". Many enzymes that act on fatty acids, as well as the corresponding Fatty-Acyl-CoAs "fatty acyl-CoAs", Fatty-Alcohols alcohols, and Fatty-Aldehydes aldehydes, have evolved to recognize substrates with a limited range of chain lengths.
