## Enriched Summary

Jasmonoyl-leucine (JA-Leu) has been detected in Arabidopsis thaliana and a number of other plant species . Two stereoisomers of this compound may exist in plants. CPD-11234 is one naturally occurring form of the compound . JA-Leu may not be a biologically active form of the hormone in all species. It does not appear to function in herbivory defense responses in TAX-49451 . The CPD-11234 isomer also does not affect seedling root growth in Arabidopsis thaliana nor does it promote JAZ/COI1 interactions in vitro . However, JA-Leu can elicit phytoalexin production in rice .
