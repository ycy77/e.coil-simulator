## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized protein YkgE While their physiological role in E. coli remains unknown, the ykgEFG genes are homologous to newly identified lactate utilization genes in Shewanella oneidensis. Low-copy expression of E. coli ykgEFG in S. oneidensis complements the ability of the Δdld-II ΔlldF double mutant for growth on L-lactate, but not D-lactate . Overexpression of ykgEFG in E. coli restores the ability of a EG10231 dld mutant to grow on D-lactate; crude extracts of an overexpressing strain contain elevated levels of both L- and D-lactate dehydrogenase activity .
