## Enriched Summary

Pterocarpan phytoalexins are antimicrobial compounds in leguminous plants formed upon pathogenic attacks and similar biotic stress-related events. Medicarpin Medicarpin, first isolated from heartwood of the tropical tree TAX-149635 , is a major pterocarpan phytoalexin. It has been reported from many legumes, including alfalfa (TAX-3879) , red clover (TAX-57577) , broad bean (TAX-3906) , all members of the TAX-78532 genus , and the model legume TAX-3880 . Medicarpin Medicarpin is found in both a (-) and a (+) conformations. While CPD-3402 appears to be the most common form, CPD-20031 has been reported from several plants such as TAX-3897 and TAX-77268 .
