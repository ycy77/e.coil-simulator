## Enriched Summary

Lycopanerols are a family of tetraterpenoid ethers related to lycopadiene. These have been isolated from the L strain of TAX-38881. They contain one to three lycopanol moieties and long chain hydrocarbons linked by ether or phenoxy bonds giving rise to high molecular weight ether lipids.
