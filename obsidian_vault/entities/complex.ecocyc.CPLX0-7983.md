## Enriched Summary

YbiO is a mechanosensitive channel of moderate conductance in E. coli K-12. In a strain lacking all seven mechanosensitive channels (MscL, MscS, MscK, MscM, YnaI, YbiO and YbdG), expression of ybiO from a multicopy plasmid results in stable channel openings in patch clamp experiments . YbiO protects cells against hypoosmotic shock when highly overexpressed . YbiO is specifically induced by NaCl and is a member of the RpoS regulon . YbiO is predicted to have 11 transmembrane segments and extensive periplasmic domains . YbiO forms homoheptameric channels . YbiO is a member of the small mechanosensitive ion channel MscS family.
