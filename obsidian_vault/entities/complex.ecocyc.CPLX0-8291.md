## Enriched Summary

HemW is a radical SAM-family protein that appears to be able to bind and then transfer heme into the NarI subunit of the respiratory enzyme NITRATREDUCTA-CPLX . Binding of a [4Fe-4S] cluster to HemW promotes dimerization of the protein; the association of heme with HemW is independent of the [4Fe-4S] cluster . HemW does not have coproporphyrinogen III dehydrogenase activity in vitro and does not complement a ΔhemN mutant. Heme-bound HemW has residual SAM cleavage activity .
