## Enriched Summary

Compounds in this class are ceramides formed from a saturated 18-carbon fatty acid (octadecanoate) and the long chain base (LCB) sphinga-(4,8)-dienine. They have a molecular formula of C36H69NO3. In plants, there is commonly a mixture of ceramides with the double bond at position 8 in the LCB in cis and trans configurations, whereas the double bond at the 4 position is generally in the trans configuration .
