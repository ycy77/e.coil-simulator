## Enriched Summary

O-acetyl-ADP-ribose (OAADPr) is produced in protein deacetylation reactions catalyzed by NAD-dependent protein deacetylases such as EC-2.3.1.286 (also known as sirtuins) , which regulate gene silencing, metabolic enzymes, and life span . OAADPr has been implicated as a signaling molecule that modulates cellular processes and harnesses the regulation of metabolism and gene activity It is accepted that the enzymatic product of sirtuins is CPD-14762. However, CPD-14762 and CPD-14763 exist in equilibrium (48:52) in solution .
