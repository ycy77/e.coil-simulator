## Enriched Summary

Brij is a registered trademark of ICI Americas, Inc, used to name a series of detergents that are ethers of polyoxyethylene with long-chain alkanes. In general, increasing the alkyl group length decreasees water solubility, while increasing the POE polymer length increases water solubility. Brij 97 is an ether of POE of about 10 oxyethylene units with an oleoyl group. It is often used for protein solubilization in biochemical applications.
