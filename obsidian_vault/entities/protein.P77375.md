Uncharacterized ferredoxin-like protein YdhX YdhX is a predicted electron transfer protein . YdhX is located in the periplasm and can be transported by both the Tat and Sec pathways . A ydhY-T mutant does not show a growth defect under anaerobic conditions, although utilization of certain sources of sulfur is impaired .
