## Enriched Summary

CPD-8204 Dimorphecolate, an unusual fatty acid derived from OLEATE-CPD by the concerted activity of two divergent desaturase enzymes, is the major fatty acid (more than 60%) found in seeds of TAX-13490 species . It has three unusual structural features: 1) a C-9 hydroxyl group 2) conjugated Δ10,Δ12 double bonds, and 3) two trans double bonds, rather than the much more common cis bonds. CPD-8204 Dimorphecolate has potential value in industrial applications such as the manufacture of paints, inks, lubricants, plastics, and nylon. Like other polyunsaturated fatty acids, dimorphecolate is not found in the seeds in a free form, but as a CPD-17286 "component of phospholipids".
