## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized protein YmgC Deletion of ymgC increases biofilm formation and motility . Expression of ymgC is regulated under conditions of biofilm formation. Deletion of tqsA (encoding the AI-2 transporter) and the presence of AI-2 or the stationary phase biofilm signal indole repress ymgC expression. ymgC is repressed in young and mature biofilms, but is induced in the intermediate, developed biofilms and by rapid acid treatment . Expression of ymgC is regulated by the repressor BluR as well as by MarA and is increased under low temperature growth conditions .
