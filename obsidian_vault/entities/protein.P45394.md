## Enriched Summary

Inner membrane protein YrbG YrbG is an uncharacterized member of the CaCA family of metal cation exchangers . Sequence similarity suggests it may function as a sodium ion/calcium ion exchanger. However direct measurement of cytosolic calcium levels using the Ca2+ activated photoprotein, aequorin, suggests that it does not play a role in regulating cytosolic Ca2+ . YrbG is predicted to be an inner membrane protein; sequence alignment and experimental topology analysis suggest that the protein has a two-domain structure each with five transmembrane segments and with opposite orientation in the membrane . yrbG is not essential for growth .
