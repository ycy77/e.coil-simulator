## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Ribosome-binding protein which interacts with both the complete 70S ribosome and the 50S subunit and is likely to be involved in ribosome assembly. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:32813020}. YggL interacts with both the intact ribosome and the 50S subunit and may be involved in ribosome assembly . yggL is transcribed during exponential growth; transcription decreases during early stationary phase. Converseley, YggL protein levels remain stable during stationary phase . A ΔyggL mutant does not show a growth defect in rich or minimal medium at 37°C, but grows significantly slower than wild type at 25°C. The mutant also contains more free 50S ribosomal subunits than wild type .
