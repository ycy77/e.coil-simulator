## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Involved in the transposition of the insertion sequence IS5. InsH is believed to be the transposase for the insertion sequence element IS5. insH spans the length of IS5 . IS5 can enhance gene transcription when it is placed on either side of the promoter for a target gene. This enhancement depends specifically on InsH and its interaction with the termini of the IS5 sequence . The consensus target for IS5 insertion is C(T/A)A(G/A) . Many copies of IS5, and thus InsH, are present in typical E. coli strains .
