## Enriched Summary

yhjB (gene.b3520) is a gene entity. It encodes yhjB (protein.P37640). Encoded protein function: Putative HTH-type transcriptional regulator YhjB EcoCyc product frame: EG12246-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 3671292-3671894. EcoCyc protein note: YhjB belongs to the NarL family of response regulators of two-component regulatory systems; no cognate histidine kinase has been identified. In vitro, YhjB enhanced the dephosphorylation of several non-cognate histidine kinase proteins . On the other hand, it was suggested that YhjB belongs to the LuxR family of transcription factors, and it was predicted to regulate genes related to metabolism . Genome-wide YhjB binding sites were determined in vivo by the chromatin immunoprecipitation method combined with lambda exonuclease digestion (ChIP-exo) in glucose M9 minimal medium . Upstream of the yhjB gene, a sequence was found that could work as a transcriptional promoter, and the transcription of the yhjB gene is affected by oxidative stress and glucose-lactose shift .
