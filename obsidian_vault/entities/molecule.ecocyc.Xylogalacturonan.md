## Enriched Summary

Xylogalacturonan "Xylogalacturonan" is a substituted 1-4-alpha-D-galacturonosyl "homogalacturonan" in which the α-1,4-linked D-GALACTURONATE backbone is substituted at position O-3 with a BETA-D-XYLOSE. Occasionally the xylose is further substituted at position O-4 with an additional β linked xylose. The homogalacturonan backbone is partially methylesterified at the C-6 carboxyl, and may be O-acetylated at positions O-2 or O-3. Xylogalacturonan "Xylogalacturonan" has been reported to be abundant in the reproductive tissues of many plants. Recently it was reported to be present in vegetative tissues of Arabidopsis .
