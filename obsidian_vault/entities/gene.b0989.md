cspH (gene.b0989) is a gene entity. It encodes cspH (protein.P0A982). Encoded protein function: Cold shock-like protein CspH (CSP-H) EcoCyc product frame: G6510-MONOMER. EcoCyc synonyms: cspK. Genomic coordinates: 1050963-1051175. EcoCyc protein note: CspH was identified as a member of the CspA cold shock protein family; it is most closely related to CspF, and its function is unknown , though recent studies are finding more conditions under which CspF and CspH respond . cspH is most highly expressed in mid-log phase; cspH expression is not cold-induced . Diverse expression responses of the cold shock proteins, CspA-I, were found under different stress conditions, specifically cold, heat, rich vs minimal media and oxidative stress caused by H2O2. Expression of cspH is significantly up in response to H2O2 stress conditions and to heat stress but is down-regulated throughout the growth cycle in both minimal and rich media and Contrary to previous reports, it does show significant up-regulation under conditions of cold shock . An in-silico comparative study of evolution, structure and function was carried out for the 9 CspA family of proteins in E. coli K-12.
