## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized HTH-type transcriptional regulator YpdC YpdC contains a predicted C-terminal AraC-type DNA-binding domain . It was predicted that this protein regulates genes related to metabolism . Upstream of the ypdC gene, a sequence was found that could work as a transcriptional promoter, and the transcription of the ypdC gene is affected by conditions that lead to biofilm formation . The YpdC binding landscape was globally mapped in vivo by standardized ChIP-seq and quantitatively modeled using BoltzNet, a biophysically informed neural network that infers binding energy from sequence data . The resulting ChIP-seq binding regions are available in RegulonDB (Galagan collection), and the corresponding energy matrix and genomic binding profiles can be accessed at boltznet.bu.edu .
