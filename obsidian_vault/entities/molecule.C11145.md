## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Methanesulfonic acid; Methanesulfonate EcoCyc common name: methanesulfonate. Methanesulfonate is a very stable strong acid and a key intermediate in the biogeochemical cycling of sulfur. It is formed in megatonne quantities in the atmosphere from the chemical oxidation of atmospheric CPD-7670 and deposited on the earth by rain and snow, and by dry deposition .
