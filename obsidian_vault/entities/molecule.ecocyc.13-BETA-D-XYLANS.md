## Enriched Summary

13-BETA-D-XYLANS "β-1,3-D-xylan" is a polysaccharide composed of β-1,3-linked BETA-D-XYLOSE residues. This polysaccharide was first found in the siphonous green seaweed TAX-148944 as a water-insoluble and alkaline-soluble component of cell walls . It was subsequently found in cell walls of some red (TAX-2784 and TAX-31346) and green (TAX-76312, TAX-3128 and TAX-170436) algae . It has not been detected in cell walls of land plants, which contain 1-4-beta-Xylan instead. Analysis of 13-BETA-D-XYLANS "β-1,3-D-xylan" from the siphonous green alga TAX-177073 showed that it is a linear heteropolysaccharide also containing glucose residues linked by β(1,3) linkages as well as some xylose residues linked by β(1,4) linkages.
