## Enriched Summary

13-14-Mixed-Xylan "Mix-linked (1→3/1→4)-β-D-xylan" is a polysaccharide composed of both β-1,3- and β-1,4-linked BETA-D-XYLOSE residues. This polysaccharide has been isolated from red algae such as the edible red seaweed TAX-2822 and members of the genus TAX-2794 (which gave it the name rhodymenan). Chemical analyses of xylans from TAX-2822 and TAX-1633773 indicated a 1:4 ratio of 1,3-linkages to 1,4 linkages .
