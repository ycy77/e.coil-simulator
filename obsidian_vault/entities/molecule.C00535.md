## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Testosterone; 17beta-Hydroxy-4-androsten-3-one Testosterone is a steroid hormone found in mammals, reptiles, birds, and other vertebrates. It is the principal male sex hormone. In mammals, testosterone is primarily secreted in the testes of males and the ovaries of females, although small amounts are also secreted by the adrenal glands. Fish make a slightly different form called CPD-13749, while insects produce ECDYSONE.
