## Enriched Summary

CPD-3571 "Propane-1,2,3-tricarboxylate", also known as tricarballylate, is considered to be the causative agent of grass tetany, a ruminant disease characterized by acute magnesium deficiency . In the rumen, CPD-225 present in grass is rapidly reduced to CPD-3571, leading to Mg(II) ion chelation and excretion and inhibition of EC-4.2.1.3 .
