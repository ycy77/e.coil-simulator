## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized protein YmdF This G0-10443 gene along with EG11127 gene is homologous to gene STM14_1829 in TAX-90371 14028s that belong to a family of proteins involved in motility . These two genes also show homology to gene PA2146 in TAX-208964, shown to contribute to both biofilm formation and susceptibility to a variety of antimicrobial agents . Deletion of both yciG and ymdF together exhibited a slight swimming motility defect . ymdF can substitute for PA2146 gene in P. aeruginosa ΔPA2146 mutants. A deletion mutant of ymdF in E. coli BW25113 was more susceptible to antimicrobial agents, such as hydrogen peroxide and ciprofloxacin and not able to form typical 3-D biofilm architecture compared to wild-type; the deletion mutant phenotype was rescued by the P. aeruginosa PA2146 gene. ymdF lacks a Walker nucleotide binding motif present in yciF; the functional significance of this difference was not examined .
