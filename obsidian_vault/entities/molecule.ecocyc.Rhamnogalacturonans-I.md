## Enriched Summary

Rhamnogalacturonans-I "Rhamnogalacturonan I" (RG-I) is a polysaccharide that represents about 20-35% of Pectin pectin. It contains a backbone consisting of repeating α-(1,2)-L-rhamnose-α-(1,4)-D-GALACTURONATE disaccharide units. Between 20 and 80% of the Rha residues are substituted with side chains of various oligosaccharides such as arabinans, galactans, and arabinogalactans. The number and type of side chains are highly variable depending on the species as well as the cell type and developmental stage.
