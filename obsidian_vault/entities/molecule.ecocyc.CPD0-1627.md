## Enriched Summary

CPD0-1627 "6-Diazo-5-oxo-L-norleucine" (DON) is the best-studied broadly active GLN antagonist, and is used as an inhibitor of enzymes that act on GLN, such as EC-3.5.1.2. The compound was originally described as a tumor-inhibitory antibiotic produced by an unidentified Streptomyces species isolated from Peruvian soil . In the same year it was also reported from TAX-66896 as a component of the tripeptide CPD-24748 . The compound was tested as a chemotherapeutic agent in several clinical studies ,but was never approved due to its toxicity . It is gaining renewed attention due to the recognition of the importance of glutaminolysis in cancer cells . The diazo group is a rare functional group that is present in some natural products, such as CPD-24753, CPD-24760, the Kinamycins kinamycins, and the Lomaiviticins lomaiviticins. The complete biosynthetic pathway of CPD-24748, including the production of CPD0-1627 from LYS, has been reported (see PWY-8293). ## Annotation

CPD0-1627 "6-Diazo-5-oxo-L-norleucine" (DON) is the best-studied broadly active GLN antagonist, and is used as an inhibitor of enzymes that act on GLN, such as EC-3.5.1.2. The compound was originally described as a tumor-inhibitory antibiotic produced by an unidentified Streptomyces species isolated from Peruvian soil . In the same year it was also reported from TAX-66896 as a component of the tripeptide CPD-24748 . The compound was tested as a chemotherapeutic agent in several clinical studies ,but was never approved due to its toxicity . It is gaining renewed attention due to the recognition of the importance of glutaminolysis in cancer cells . The diazo group is a rare functional group that is present in some natural products, such as CPD-24753, CPD-24760, the Kinamycins kinamycins, and the Lomaiviticins lomaiviticins. The complete biosynthetic pathway of CPD-24748, including the production of CPD0-1627 from LYS, has been reported (see PWY-8293).
