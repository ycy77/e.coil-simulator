Inner membrane protein YnbA YnbA is an inner membrane protein with five predicted transmembrane domains. The C terminus is located in the cytoplasm . Based on sequence similarity, YnbA was predicted to be a diacylglycerol cholinephosphotransferase ; however, phosphatidylcholine is not a component of E. coli membranes . ynbA was identified in a screen for genes affecting Fe-S cluster biogenesis and/or iron homeostasis . Expression of ynbA is activated by BglJ-RcsB .
