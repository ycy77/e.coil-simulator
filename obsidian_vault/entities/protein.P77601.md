Putative HTH-type transcriptional regulator YkgA YkgA is similar to the transcription activators MarA, SoxS, and Rob. Overexpression of YkgA does not induce the expression of the tested mar, sox and rob regulon promoters, but does cause a slow growth phenotype . YkgA may be the product of a pseudogene .
