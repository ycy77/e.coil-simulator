## Enriched Summary

The peptidoglycan of TAX-971, a strictly anaerobic bacterium, contains CADAVERINE, which is linked covalently to the α nitrogen of the D-GLT residue . The CADAVERINE is necessary for the normal growth of the bacterium. Inactivation of EC-4.1.1.18, which produces the cadaverine from LYS, results in abnormal cell structure and cell lysis .
