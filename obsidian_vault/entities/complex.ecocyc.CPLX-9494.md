## Enriched Summary

The holocytochrome c synthase complex consists of EG12052 CcmH, which is involved in the reduction of the cysteine residues of the apocytochrome, and EG12054 CcmF, which is responsible for reduction of the heme molecule and its attachment to one of the reduced cysteines of the apocytochrome. The reduction of both the cysteine residues and the heme's iron is essential for attachment.
