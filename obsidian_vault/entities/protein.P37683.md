## Enriched Summary

Inner membrane protein YiaV YiaV is predicted to be an inner membrane protein with two transmembrane domains; experimental topology analysis suggests the C terminus is located in the periplasm . YiaV has also been predicted to be a bitopic (single pass) inner membrane protein . YiaV is a member of the Membrane Fusion Protein (MFP) Family A transposon insertion in the yiaW-aldB intergenic region has higher tolerance to kanamycin, while a mutant lacking that region is more sensitive to kanamycin than wild type. A yiaV deletion has no effect on kanamycin sensitivity .
