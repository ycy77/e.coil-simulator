## Enriched Summary

It is a metabolite of Penicillium citreoviride thought to be the cause of yellow rice poisoning, manifested in humans and experimental animals by respiratory and circulatory failure, paralysis, convulsions and death. It is identical to beriberi, originally considered to be a thiamin nutritional deficiency.
