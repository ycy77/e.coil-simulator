## Enriched Summary

This class of macrolide antibiotics includes several wide-spectrum macrolide antibiotics obtained from the actinomycete TAX-1836 (formerly known as Streptomyces erythraeus). The antibiotic Erythromycins erythramycin binds to the 50S ribosomal subunit precursor, as well as the mature form of the 50S subunit of the bacterial 70S rRNA complex, inhibiting its synthesis and mRNA translation, and thus stopping structural and functional processes that are critical for life or replication.
