## Enriched Summary

A heterocyclic aldehyde derived from agricultural byproducts such as wheat bran and saw dust. Also produced during the hydrolysis of pentose polymers in hemicellulose . Furfural is a toxic compound to many microorganisms, which have an array of oxidoreductases that can reduce furfural to a less toxic furfural alcohol.
