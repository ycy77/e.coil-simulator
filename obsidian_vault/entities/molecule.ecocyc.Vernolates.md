## Enriched Summary

Vernolates Vernolate (vernolic acid) is a fatty acid with an 18-carbon chain that contains a cis double bond at position 9 and an epoxy group at positions 12-13. Like other fatty acids, Vernolates is rarely found in its free form. It is usually found Glycerolipid-vernolate "incorporated into a lipid". When studied in planrts, the compound is usually referred to as "vernolic acid". When studied in mammals, it is referred to as "isoleukotoxin".
