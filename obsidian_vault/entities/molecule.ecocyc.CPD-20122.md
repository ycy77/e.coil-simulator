## Enriched Summary

CPD-20122 Palatinose, also known as isomaltulose, is a reducing disaccharide of about one-third the sweetness of sucrose, that is very resistant to hydrolysis by acid and by EC-3.2.1.26. It has very similar physical properties to sucrose, and is found naturally in small quantities in honey .
