## Enriched Summary

CPD-13962 Daunorubicin is Anthracyclines antibiotic produced by some strains of TAX-1950 and other streptomycetes. It was discovered in the 1960s , and was widely used as as antitumor drug. However, by 1967 it was recognized that it could produce fatal cardiac toxicity. Today analogs of the natural antibiotic are used as drugs. Over 2,000 analogs have been developed. The polyketide derived framework of daunorubicin is made from propanoyl-CoA as the starter unit and malonyl-CoA for the extender units. It contains the unusual deoxyaminosugar CPD-13971. CPD-13962 Daunorubicin was shown to be hydroxylated to CPD-13973 in vivo in TAX-1950 .
