## Enriched Summary

DdpABCDF is a putative D,D-dipeptide ABC transporter that may function to import D-alanyl-D-alanine for use as an energy source under starvation conditions . ddpA-D and ddpF are located in a gene cluster with ddpX which encodes a zinc-dependent D-alanyl-D-alanine dipeptidase; the ddp gene cluster is expressed during stationary phase . E. coli K-12 BW25113 is not able to grow on D-alanyl-D-alanine as the sole carbon source unless ddpX is overexpressed .
