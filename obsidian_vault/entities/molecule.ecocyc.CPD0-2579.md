## Enriched Summary

This compound represents a mature peptidoglycan polymer (meso-diaminopimelate containing) that has been released from its site of biosynthesis on the outer face of the inner membrane. It contains a D,D cross link formed between the carboxyl group of D-alanine in position 4 of a peptide stem on one glycan chain and the amino group at the D-center of meso-DAP from a separate glycan chain. While the first chain is always a tetrapeptide (the fifth residue, a D-alanine, is released during formation of the cross-link), the second chain may be a pentapeptide, a tetrapeptide, or a tripeptide, depending on prior modifications.
