## Enriched Summary

ydjI (gene.b1773) is a gene entity. It encodes ydjI (protein.P77704). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: Aldolase that catalyzes the reversible condensation of an array of aldose sugars with dihydroxyacetone phosphate (DHAP) to form carbohydrates (PubMed:31322866). It shows a strong preference for higher-order (seven-, eight- and nine-carbon) monosaccharides with specific hydroxyl stereochemistries and a negatively charged terminus (carboxylate or phosphate) (PubMed:31322866). The best substrate in vitro is L-glycero-L-galacto-octuluronate-1-phosphate, which is cleaved into DHAP and L-arabinuronate (PubMed:31322866). L-glycero-L-galacto-octuluronate-1-phosphate is probably not the natural substrate, but it is highly likely that the physiological substrate has a very similar structure (Probable). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:31322866, ECO:0000305|PubMed:31322866}.
