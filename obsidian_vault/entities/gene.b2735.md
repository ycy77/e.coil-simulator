YgbI DNA-binding activity was probed in vivo in glucose M9 minimal medium through chromatin immunoprecipitation assays (ChIP-exo), which identified a single binding region in between the ygbI and ygbJK divergent transcription units . By RNA-seq of the wild-type and ygbI knockout strains, it was determined that transcription of ygbJ and ygbK is negatively regulated by YgbI . In conjunction, ChIP-exo and RNA-seq studies indicated that ygbJK is a direct target of YgbI . Upstream of the ygbI gene, a sequence was found that could work as a transcriptional promoter, and the transcription of the ygbI gene is affected by glucose-lactose shift and heat shock .
