## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Acts as a chaperone that increases YcdX activity, maybe by facilitating the correct insertion of the zinc ions into the catalytic site of YcdX. Involved in the swarming motility process. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21965574}. YcdY is a member of an atypical subfamily of TorD chaperones . YcdY appears to enable maturation of YcdX, potentially by facilitating insertion of Zn2+ into the catalytic site of YcdX . Phylogenetic analysis of the NarJ subfamily of proteins suggests that YcdY has only recently evolved from DmsD . A ycdY mutant has a swarming defect . Review:
