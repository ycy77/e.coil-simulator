## Enriched Summary

PTS enzyme I is one of two sugar-non-specific protein constituents of the phosphoenolpyruvate:sugar phosphotransferase system (PTS). It accepts a phosphoryl group from PHOSPHO-ENOL-PYRUVATE and transfers it to the other sugar-non-specific protein, HPr (histidine protein), which in turn transfers it to the EIIA domain of any one of the many sugar-specific enzymes (collectively known as Enzymes II) of the PTS.
