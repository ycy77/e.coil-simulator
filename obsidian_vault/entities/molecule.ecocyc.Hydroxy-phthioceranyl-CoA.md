## Enriched Summary

This class stands for the acyl-CoA form of either Phthioceranic-Acids "phthioceranic acids" or Hydroxyphthioceranic-Acids "hydroxyphthioceranic acids". In heptamethylated phthioceranic acids, R1,R2,R3 = H. In octamethylated phthioceranic acids, R1,R3 = H, R2=CH3. In heptamethylated hydroxyphthioceranic acids, R2,R3 = H, R1=OH. In octamethylated hydroxyphthioceranic acids, R1= H, R2=CH3, and R3=OH.
