## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized protein YmdA Expression of YmdA is posttranscriptionally activated by CsrA. CsrA binds to two sites in the 5' UTR of ymdA; both binding sites are required to destabilize a hairpin structure that sequesters the ribosome binding site . Overexpression of YmdA inhibits biofilm formation . A ΔymdA strain shows a modest decrease in biofilm formation .
