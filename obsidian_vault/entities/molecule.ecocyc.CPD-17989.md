## Enriched Summary

This compound represents a peptidoglycan polymer (meso-DAP containing) that has been released from its site of synthesis at the outer face of the inner membrane. The R group represents a glycan chain of β1,4-linked N-acetylglucosamine (GlcNAc) and N-acetylmuramic acid (MurNAc) residues with peptide stems of varying length (penta-, tetra- and tripeptide) - some of which may be involved in cross-links with other glycan strands. One end of the released strand will terminate with 1,6-anyhdroMurNAc (shown here), the other end (not shown here) will terminate with a GlcNAc residue .
