## Enriched Summary

CPD-4461 Plumbagin (5-hydroxy-2-methyl-1,4-naphthoquinone) is a yellow colored pigment that accumulates in the roots of TAX-122308. It was first isolated from TAX-122308, and subsequently identified in a number of microbes and other plants. CPD-4461 Plumbagin has several pharmacological uses as an antimicrobial, antimalarial and anticancer agent. Its action on non-small lung cells has been demonstrated . Plumbagin's role as an antiproliferative agent shows that it disrupts the microtubule network by binding tubulin . It is considered a promising chemotherapeutic candidate for breast cancer .
