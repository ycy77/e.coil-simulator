HEPARIN "Heparin" is a polymer mainly composed of a repeating trisulfated disaccharide unit composed of CPD-12518 and CPD-12533 linked by a (1->4) bond (often referred to as IdoA(2S)-(1->4)-GlcNS(6S)). Consecutive glycosidic linkages formed between C1 of CPD-12533 in one base unit and C4 of CPD-12518 of another unit result in the formation of the heparin polymer.
