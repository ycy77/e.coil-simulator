## Enriched Summary

Polysialic acids are polymers of N-ACETYLNEURAMINATE "sialic acid" and its derivatives. The degree of polymerization is 8 to 200 Sia residues. Polysialic acid is an unusual posttranslational modification that occurs on the neural cell adhesion molecules (NCAMs).
