## Enriched Summary

Aggrecan is a proteoglycan, a core protein modified with carbohydrates. The human core protein, encoded by the gene ACAN, is very large (over 250 kDa). Along with Type-II collagen, aggrecan forms a major structural component of cartilage, particularly articular cartilage. Aggrecan consists of two globular structural domains at the N-terminal end and one globular domain at the C-terminal end, separated by a large domain heavily modified with glycosaminoglycans. The two main modifier moieties are themselves arranged into distinct regions, a Chondroitin-sulfates region and a KERATAN-6-SULFATE region.
