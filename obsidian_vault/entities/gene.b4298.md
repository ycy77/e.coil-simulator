## Enriched Summary

yjhH (gene.b4298) is a gene entity. It encodes yjhH (protein.P39359). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: Functions as a 2-dehydro-3-deoxy-D-pentonate aldolase. {ECO:0000305|PubMed:23233208}. EcoCyc product frame: G7911-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 4524105-4525010. EcoCyc protein note: YjhH appears to be a 2-dehydro-3-deoxy-D-pentonate aldolase . A yjhH yagE double mutant can not use D-xylonate as the sole source of carbon, and crude cell extracts do not contain 2-dehydro-3-deoxy-D-pentonate aldolase activity. Both phenotypes are complemented by providing yjhH on a plasmid . Overexpression of yjhH has been used for metabolic engineering of glycolate production . ArcA appears to activate yjhH gene expression under anaerobiosis. Two putative ArcA binding sites were identified 211 and 597 bp upstream of this gene , but no promoter upstream of it has been identified.
