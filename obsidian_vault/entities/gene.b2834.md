tas (gene.b2834) is a gene entity. It encodes tas (protein.P0A9T4). Encoded protein function: Protein tas EcoCyc product frame: G7462-MONOMER. EcoCyc synonyms: ygdS. Genomic coordinates: 2971597-2972637. EcoCyc protein note: Tas is a protein with similarity to aldo-keto reductases . The physiological substrate is unknown. A crystal structure of Tas has been solved, showing a canonical (β/α)8 barrel with an NADP(H) molecule bound to the protein . Re-evaluation of the structural data led to suggest that the protein is a homodimer. In experiments with an auxotrophic E. coli B/r strain, Tas was required for starvation-associated mutational suppression of a tyrA14(oc) allele. Multicopy tas expression suppressed the tyrosine auxotrophy of a tyrA14(oc) leu308(am) mutant, which has a prephenate dehydrogenase defect . Sequence differences between E. coli strains B and K-12 have been noted . Tas: "tyrosine auxotrophy suppressor" Review:
