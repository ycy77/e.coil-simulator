## Enriched Summary

The mannan found in the cell walls of TAX-4932 is composed of a linear chain of Unbranched-1-6-Mannan "α-(1,6)-D-mannan" decorated by side chains and attached to ASN residues of proteins by a linkage unit consisting of two β-(1,4)-N-acetylglucosamine residues. The composition of the side chains varies, but largely consists of α-(1,3)-mannosyl-α-(1,2)-mannosyl-α-(1,2)-mannosyl chains. The side chain can be shorter, and can be branched as well. This structure results from the action of EC-3.2.1.198, which cleaves CPD-12789 off the side chains, leaving single α(1,2)-mannose residues attached to the backbone.
