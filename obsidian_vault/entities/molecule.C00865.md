## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Phospholipid EcoCyc common name: a phospholipid. Phospholipids are a class of phosphorous-containing lipids that are composed mainly of fatty acids, a phosphate group, and a backbone made of a simple organic molecule. Almost all of the phospholipids have a glycerol backbone. Such a phospholipid is known as Phosphoglycerides. Some types of phospholipids, for example, Sphingomyelins, have a backbone made of the amino-alcohol sphingosine. Phospholipids are a major component of all biological membranes.
