## Enriched Summary

The angucyclines are a group of aromatic polyketide natural products from microbial sources of which the first member was discovered in 1966. The classification of the angucyclines is linked to the tetracyclic benz[a]anthracene system. The angucyclines display anticancer activity as well as a multitude of other activities such as antibacterial, antiviral, and enzyme inhibitory properties. Most of them are glycoconjugates containing one or more sugars attached at different positions of the angucyclinone framework .
