## Enriched Summary

Hydroxycinnamic acids are abundant phenolic CPD-674 "cinnamate" derivatives synthesized in plants. They are rarely found in free form within plant material, and usually occur linked to various carbohydrates as glycosidic conjugates, and as various esters and amides within a wide variety of natural products, such as lignin .
