## Enriched Summary

CPD-20899 Valinomycin is a cyclooligomer Depsipeptides depsipeptide produced by several Streptomyces strains . It has diverse biological activities including antifungal , antibacterial , insecticidal-nematocidal , antiparasitic , antiviral , and antitumor activities. Of particular interest is that valinomycin demonstrated a high potency against SARS-CoV in infected Vero E6 cells with an EC50 of 0.85 μm . CPD-20899 Valinomycin forms an ionophore that specifically modulates potassium ion transport across biological membranes, which may explain some of its biological activity . CPD-20899 Valinomycin is a dodecadepsipeptide assembled from 3 identical copies of the tetradepsipeptide CPD-25315, which contains alternating amide and ester bonds . Interestingly, a very similar compound produced by TAX-1892, CPD-25325, is a cyclic octadepsipeptide consisting of two copies of the same tetradepsipeptide . ## Annotation

CPD-20899 Valinomycin is a cyclooligomer Depsipeptides depsipeptide produced by several Streptomyces strains . It has diverse biological activities including antifungal , antibacterial , insecticidal-nematocidal , antiparasitic , antiviral , and antitumor activities. Of particular interest is that valinomycin demonstrated a high potency against SARS-CoV in infected Vero E6 cells with an EC50 of 0.85 μm . CPD-20899 Valinomycin forms an ionophore that specifically modulates potassium ion transport across biological membranes, which may explain some of its biological activity . CPD-20899 Valinomycin is a dodecadepsipeptide assembled from 3 identical copies of the tetradepsipeptide CPD-25315, which contains alternating amide and ester bonds . Interestingly, a very similar compound produced by TAX-1892, CPD-25325, is a cyclic octadepsipeptide consisting of two copies of the same tetradepsipeptide . The biosynthesis of CPD-20899 is described in PWY-8336.
