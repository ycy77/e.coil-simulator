## Enriched Summary

Chrysolaminarin is a linear polymer of (1→3) and (1→6) β-linked D-glucose units in a ratio of 11:1 . Chrysolaminarin is a storage polysaccharide typically found in photosynthetic TAX-33634 (heterokonts). It is used as a carbohydrate food reserve by phytoplankton such as TAX-2836 (diatoms), and is one of the most common biopolymers in the world.
