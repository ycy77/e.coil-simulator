## Enriched Summary

CPD-26208 Glycerophosphoinositol (GPI) is an ionic compatible solute only found thus far in hyperthermophilic bacteria and archaea of the genera TAX-2713 and TAX-2233, respectively . Different stereoisomers of this compound accumulate in archaea and bacteria, respectively. In bacteria, the first enzyme of the pathway, EC-2.7.7.39, has a storng or absolute preference for GLYCEROL-3P, leading to production of CPD-541. In archaea, the comparable enzyme has a preference for SN-GLYCEROL-1-PHOSPHATE, resulting in formation of CPD-26207 .
