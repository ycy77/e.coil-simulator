## Enriched Summary

CPD-16617 "Pantethine" (bis-pantethine or co-enzyme pantethine) is a dimeric form of PANTOTHENATE (vitamin B5) linked by cysteamine bridging groups. The monomer of this compound is CPD-511 and is an intermediate in the production of CO-A by the body. Pantethine is considered the more biologically active form of vitamin B5, but it is less stable. Most vitamin B5 supplements are in the form of calcium pantothenate, a salt of PANTOTHENATE.
