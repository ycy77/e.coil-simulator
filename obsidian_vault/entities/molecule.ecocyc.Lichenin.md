## Enriched Summary

Lichenin Lichenan is a complex glucan occurring in certain species of lichens, consisting of glucosyl residues interconnected by β-1,3 and β-1,4 linkages. It is an important carbohydrate for reindeers and northern flying squirrels, which eat lichen.
