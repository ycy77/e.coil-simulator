## Enriched Summary

Despite its similarity to PYRIDOXAL_PHOSPHATE (PLP), the active form of vitamin B6, CPD0-1605 cannot be used as a source of vitamin B6 biosynthesis. Rather, it interferes with vitamin B6 homeostasis by acting as an extremely strong competitive inhibitor of EC-2.7.1.35. In addition, the phosphorylated form CPD0-1221 inhibits PLP-dependent enzymes .
