## Enriched Summary

Xyloglucan oligosaccharides are produced from Xyloglucan xyloglucan by the action of endo- or exo-(1,4)-β-glucanases. A study that analyzed the products of tamarind xyloglucan digestion by several endoglucanases from different organisms found that all enzymes produced oligosaccharides with the composition CPD-13375 XXXG, CPD-13376 XXLG/CPD-13377 XLXG and CPD-13378 XLLG as the end products of xyloglucan hydrolysis (see Xyloglucan for explanation of this terminology). Xyloglucan from TAX-3702 is comprised of repeating subunits of CPD-13418 XXFG, CPD-13375 XXXG, CPD-13376 XXLG, CPD-13417 XLFG, CPD-13378 XLLG, and CPD-13377 XLXG .
