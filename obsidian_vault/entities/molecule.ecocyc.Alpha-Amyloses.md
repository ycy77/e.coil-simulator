## Enriched Summary

Starch Starch is a mixture of the two polymeric carbohydrates (polysaccharides) Alpha-Amyloses and CPD-7043. Alpha-Amyloses "α-Amylose" contains up to several thousand glucosyl units linked almost exclusively in linear α(1->4) linkages, with very few branches of α(1->6) linkages. In CPD-7043, on the other hand, branching is common, and occurs approximately every 24-30 glucose units. Alpha-Amyloses "α-Amylose" accounts for 30% of starch, while CPD-7043 accounts for the other 70%.
