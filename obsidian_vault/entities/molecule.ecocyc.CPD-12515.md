## Enriched Summary

CPD-12515 is the major uronic acid component of the glycosaminoglycans Dermatan-Sulfate "dermatan sulfate" and HEPARIN. It is also present in Heparan-Sulfate, but in a minor amount relative to its carbon-5 epimer GLUCURONATE. CPD-12515 is often modified by the addition of an O-sulfate group at carbon position 2 to form CPD-12518.
