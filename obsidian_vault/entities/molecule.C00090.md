KEGG compound: Catechol; 1,2-Benzenediol; o-Benzenediol; 1,2-Dihydroxybenzene; Brenzcatechin; Pyrocatechol Catechol is the ortho isomer of the three isomeric Benzenediols benzenediols: CATECHOL (benzene-1,2-diol), CPD-623 (benzene-1,3-diol), and HYDROQUINONE (benzene-1,4-diol). About 20,000 tonnes of catechol are produced annually as a commodity organic chemical, mainly as a precursor to pesticides, flavors, and fragrances. The anion form that is the conjugate base of catechol is called catecholate.
