## Enriched Summary

In this reaction nitrate/nitrite response regulator NarL is phosphorylated by the phospho-NarQ sensor protein. In the presence of nitrate the nitrate/nitrite response regulator NarL can be phosphorylated by both nitrate/nitrite sensor kinase-phosphotransferases phospho-NarX and phospho-NarQ. In this activated state phospho-NarL can act as both an activator of nitrate and nitrite reductase transcription and repressor of fumarate reductase transcription. Both actions switch anaerobic respiration to utilization of either nitrate or nitrite as electron acceptors. In the presence of nitrite the NarL protein is phosphorylated only by phospho-NarQ. The sensor protein NarX acts as a phospho-NarL phosphatase under these conditions, countering the sensor protein NarQ phosphorylation to some extent.
