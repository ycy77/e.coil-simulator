## Enriched Summary

yfaL (gene.b2233) is a gene entity. It encodes yfaL (protein.P45508). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: Probably an autotransporter (PubMed:16522795, PubMed:22344647). Upon overexpression shows increased adherence to polyvinyl chloride (PVC) plates, increased mature biofilm formation (PubMed:15659678). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15659678, ECO:0000305|PubMed:16522795, ECO:0000305|PubMed:22344647}. EcoCyc product frame: EG12850-MONOMER. EcoCyc synonyms: yfaF, yfaK, yfaJ. Genomic coordinates: 2340417-2344169. EcoCyc protein note: yfaL has homology with G7080 flu - encoding the autotransporter (AT) self-recognizing adhesin Ag43; deletion of yfaL has no significant effect on adhesion properties in microtiter plate assays or on biofilm formation however increased expression of yfaL results in increased adhesion and increased biofilm formation compared to the wild-type; overexpression of yfaL promotes bacterial adhesion to microtiter PVC plates to a greater level than overexpression of flu . A yfaL deletion mutant is 6-fold more sensitive to UV-radiation than wild type . yfaL exhibits mosaic evolution patterns in E. coli .
