## Enriched Summary

Succinylarginine dihydrolase (AstB) catalyzes the second reaction in the ammonia-producing arginine catabolic pathway, AST-PWY . In this unusual reaction the guanidino group of succinylarginine is completely degraded. AstB is a member of the amidinotransferase superfamily of enzymes . Crystal structures of AstB have been solved, and a reaction mechanism was proposed . Expression of the enzymes of the AST pathway is regulated by arginine and nitrogen availability via ArgR and NtrC .
