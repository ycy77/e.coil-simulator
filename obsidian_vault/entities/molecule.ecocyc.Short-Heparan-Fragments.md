## Enriched Summary

Heparan sulfate is a member of the Glycosaminoglycans "glycosaminoglycan" family. It consists of a variably sulfated repeating disaccharide unit which is composed of a hexuronic acid and a hexosamine. The most common repeating unit, which makes up around 50% of the total disaccharide units in heparan sulfate, is composed of CPD-12521 linked to CPD-12541.
