## Enriched Summary

ghxQ (gene.b4464) is a gene entity. It encodes ghxQ (protein.Q46817). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: High-affinity transporter for guanine and hypoxanthine. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:24214977}. EcoCyc product frame: G7504-MONOMER. EcoCyc synonyms: b2885, ygfR, b2884, ygfQ. Genomic coordinates: 3027121-3028488. EcoCyc protein note: GhxQ mediates the uptake of guanine and hypoxanthine (but not adenine, xanthine, uric acid nor uracil) when overexpressed in strains (ΔyjcD or ΔpurP) which lack significant background activity; GhxQ transports guanine and hypoxanthine with high affinity . GhxQ is a member of the nucleobase:cation symporter 2 (NCS2) family of transporters . GhxQ is part of a putative purine catablic operon . Expression of ghxQ is significantly induced under acidic growth conditions (pH 5.5 and 4.5) . ghxQ: guanine / hypoxanthine permease Q
