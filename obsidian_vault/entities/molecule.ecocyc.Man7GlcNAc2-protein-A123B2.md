## Enriched Summary

This is one of four isomers of Man7GlcNAc2 chains that are found in N glycans. The nomenclature used to identify high mannose glycans has not been standardized. Several earlier systems were insufficient to describe all possible isomers . The nomenclature used here is that proposed by Prien et al , which can represent all processing isomers.
