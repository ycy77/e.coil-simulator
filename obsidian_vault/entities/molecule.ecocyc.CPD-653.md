## Enriched Summary

(R)-NADHX is a hydrated form of NADH, where the hydroxyl group is attached at position 6 of the nicotinamide moiety in (S) configuration. Studies in the 1950's have shown that EC-1.2.1.12, slowly catalyzes the formation of NADHX from NADH . The equilibrium of this hydration reaction is in favor (100/1) of the hydrated form , making the reaction virtually irreversible. NADHX and NADPHX are inhibitors of several dehydrogenases and it is thus important to eliminate them. Reconversion of NADHX to NADH is catalyzed by an ENSG00000153481-MONOMER "ATP-dependent dehydratase" .
