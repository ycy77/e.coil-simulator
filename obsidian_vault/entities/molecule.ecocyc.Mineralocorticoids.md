## Enriched Summary

Mineralocorticoids are a class of steroid hormones that regulate water and electrolyte metabolism. The primary endogenous mineralocorticoid is aldosterone. The name glucocorticoid is derived from an early observations that these hormones are involved in the retention of the mineral sodium.
