## Enriched Summary

L-Iduronate is a reducing acidic sugar that can form a ring structure, as shown for CPD-12515. It is the major uronic acid component of the glycosaminoglycans Dermatan-Sulfate "dermatan sulfate" and HEPARIN. It is also present in Heparan-Sulfate, but in a minor amount relative to its carbon-5 epimer GLUCURONATE. L-Iduronate is often modified by the addition of an O-sulfate group at C-2 to form CPD-12518.
