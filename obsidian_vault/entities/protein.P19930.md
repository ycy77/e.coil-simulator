## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Protease involved in the C-terminal processing of HyaB, the large subunit of hydrogenase 1. {ECO:0000305}. The HyaD protein is required for the synthesis of active hydrogenase isoenzyme 1 (HYD1) . HyaD is similar to HybD, the maturation protease for hydrogenase 2 . It is therefore likely that HyaD is an endopeptidase required for maturation of hydrogenase 1.
