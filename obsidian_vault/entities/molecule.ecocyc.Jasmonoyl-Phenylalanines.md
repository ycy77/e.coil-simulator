## Enriched Summary

Jasmonoyl-phenylalanine (JA-Phe) has been detected in TAX-3702 . Two stereoisomers of this compound exist in plants - CPD-11235 and CPD-19817 . It has been reported that, unlike JA-Ile, JA-Phe does not promote interaction between the JAZ1/JAZ3 and COI1 proteins from tomato in vitro. However, the compound used was mixture of synthetic (+)-JA-Phe and (-)-JA-Phe diastereomers, and did not include the naturally-occuring CPD-19817 . CPD-11235 [(-)-JA-L-Phe] was tested for its activity in TAX-3702 using three different assays, and did not show any biological activity (supplementary materials). On the other hand, (-)-JA-L-Phe was reported to elicit strongly the biosynthesis of the phytoalexin CPD-7079 in rice . It is possible that the other stereoisomer, CPD-19817, might have some biological activity (as is the case for CPD-11259 vs. CPD-11232) . JA-Phe and its hydroxylated derivatives have been shown to accumulate in TAX-3702 following leaf wounding or fungal infection, although the stereochemistry has not been taken into account in this study as well .
