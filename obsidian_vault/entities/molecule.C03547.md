## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: omega-Hydroxy fatty acid EcoCyc common name: an ω-hydroxy fatty acid. An ω-hydroxy fatty acid is Fatty-Acids with a hydroxy group at the end of the chain that is farthest from the carboxylated end. Fatty acids are often classified into four groups by their lengths: Short-chain-fatty-acids "Short-chain fatty acids" (SCFA) are fatty acids with aliphatic chains of fewer than six carbons. Medium-chain-fatty-acids "Medium-chain fatty acids" (MCFA) are fatty acids with aliphatic chain of 6-12 carbons. Long-Chain-Fatty-Acids "Long-chain fatty acids" (LCFA) are fatty acids with aliphatic chains of 13-22 carbons. Very-long-chain-fatty-acids "Very-long-chain fatty acids" (VLCFA) are fatty acids with aliphatic chains of 23 carbons or longer.
