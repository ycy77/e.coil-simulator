## Enriched Summary

Homocysteine editing by methionyl-tRNA synthetase results in the formation of homocysteine thiolactone. In addition to being cleared from the circulation by urinary excretion, homocysteine thiolactone is detoxified by the serum homocysteine thiolactonase/paraoxonase carried on high density lipoprotein, and inside cells by the enzyme known as EC-3.4.22.40 .
