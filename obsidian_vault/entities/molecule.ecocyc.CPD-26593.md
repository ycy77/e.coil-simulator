## Enriched Summary

Carmofur is derivative of CPD0-1327 and an approved antineoplastic drug used to treat colorectal cancer. It is a potent inhibitor of human acid ceramidase , of human fatty acid amide hydrolase (FAAH), and of bacterial N-acylethanolamine acid amidase . It has bee demonstrated in a structural study to inhibit the SARS-CoV-2 main protease and consequently inhibit viral replication in cells , and was reported to be a weak allosteric inhibitor of SAH nucleosidase .
