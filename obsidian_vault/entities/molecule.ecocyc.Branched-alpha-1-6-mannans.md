## Enriched Summary

The mannan found in the cell walls of TAX-4932 is composed of a linear chain of α-(1,6)-D-mannan decorated by side chains and attached to ASN residues of proteins by a linkage unit consisting of two β-(1,4)-N-acetylglucosamine residues. The composition of the side chains varies, but largely consists of α-(1,3)-mannosyl-α-(1,2)-mannosyl-α-(1,2)-mannosyl chains. The side chain can be shorter, and can be branched as well. For the sake of simplicity, the structure shown here shows only a single side chain. In the actual polymer almost all of the residues of the backbone are decorated with side chains.
