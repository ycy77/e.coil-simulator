## Enriched Summary

Nascent peptidoglycan molecules are attached to a polyprenol membrane anchor via a diphosphate bridge. In most organisms the anchor is undecaprenol, although variations do occur. For example, in Mycobacteria the anchor is decaprenol. The structure seen here shows a typical composition but many variations exist.
