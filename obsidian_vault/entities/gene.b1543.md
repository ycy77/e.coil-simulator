ydfJ (gene.b1543) is a gene entity. It encodes ydfJ (protein.P77228). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: When overexpressed in human HEK-293 cells forms an inward rectifying potassium channel. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21744086}. EcoCyc product frame: B1543-MONOMER. EcoCyc synonyms: ydfJ_2. Genomic coordinates: 1631002-1632285. EcoCyc protein note: The YdfJ protein is an uncharacterised member of the Metabolite:H+ Symporter (MHS) family within the Major Facilitator Superfamily (MFS) of transporters . Heterologous expression of cloned ydfJ (expressed in the mammalian cell line, HEK-293) produces a functional K+ channel . ydfJ may constitute the C-terminal portion of an ancestral gene disrupted by an insertion of the cryptic prophage Qin/Kim. The N-terminal fragment may be G0-10559 ynfP.
