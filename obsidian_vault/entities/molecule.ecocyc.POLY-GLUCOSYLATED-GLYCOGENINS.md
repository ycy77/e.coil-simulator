## Enriched Summary

This class includes the precursors of glycogen that are substrates for the enzyme CPLX-6975. It includes poly-glycosylated glycogenin (including CPD-7010, also known as the glycogen primer), but not GLUCOSYL-GLYCOGENIN, which can not serve as a primer for CPLX-6975.
