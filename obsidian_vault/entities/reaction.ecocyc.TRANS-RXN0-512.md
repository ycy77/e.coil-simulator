## Enriched Summary

Auxotrophy for diaminopimelic acid (DAP) can be complemented by exogenous cystathionine. L-cystathionine, supplied in the growth media of a dapAmetC mutant strain replaces meso-DAP in the peptidoglycan . Analysis of E. coli K-12 on Biolog microplates indicates that L-cystathionine is able to serve as the sole source of sulfur. The transporter responsible has not been identified.
