## Enriched Summary

Mannans are polymers composed of MANNOSE residues. Pure mannans are rarely found, and most contain α(1,6)-linked galactopyranosyl side chains. However, in certain filamentous fungi galactofuranose (Galf) is found in galactomannan instead of galactopyranose. The galactomannan attached to N-glycans in TAX-746128 is composed of a linear mannan core comprising CPD-20116 "α-(1-2)-linked mannotetraose" repeating units attached to each other via α-(1-6) linkages and decorated by side chains of β-(1-5)-galactofuranose units, which are linked to the C2 position of the non-reducing mannose residue in each mannotetraose unit . The length of the side chains depends on growth conditions. Cells grown in YPD medium produced galactomannan with side chain consisting an average of 3-4 units of galactofuranose , while cells grown in YNB medium incorporated on average 10 Galf units that may contain one β-(1,6) linkage midchain . It should be noted that a different form of galactomannan containing galactofuranose resides is found in O-glycans produced by this organism, in which the mannan core is much shorter, consisting of only one to two mannosyl residues . ## Annotation

Mannans are polymers composed of MANNOSE residues. Pure mannans are rarely found, and most contain α(1,6)-linked galactopyranosyl side chains. However, in certain filamentous fungi galactofuranose (Galf) is found in galactomannan instead of galactopyranose. The galactomannan attached to N-glycans in TAX-746128 is composed of a linear mannan core comprising CPD-20116 "α-(1-2)-linked mannotetraose" repeating units attached to each other via α-(1-6) linkages and decorated by side chains of β-(1-5)-galactofuranose units, which are linked to the C2 position of the non-reducing mannose residue in each mannotetraose unit . The length of the side chains depends on growth conditions. Cells grown in YPD medium produced galactomannan with side chain consisting an average of 3-4 units of galactofuranose , while cells grown in YNB medium incorporated on average 10 Galf units that may contain one β-(1,6) linkage midchain . It should be noted that a different form of galactomannan containing galactofuranose resides is found in O-glycans produced by this organism, in which the mannan core is much shorter, consisting of only one to two mannosyl residues .
