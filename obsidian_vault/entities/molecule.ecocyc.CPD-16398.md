## Enriched Summary

The osmoregulated periplasmic glucans (OPGs) of E. coli K-12, formerly called membrane derived oligosaccharides (MDOs), are highly branched structures. The backbone consists of β-1,2 linked glucose units to which β-1,6 linked branches are attached. OPGs range in size from 5 - 12 molecules. The OPG backbone can also be modifed by phosphoglycerol, succinyl and/or phosphoethanolamine substituents.
