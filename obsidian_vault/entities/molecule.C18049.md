## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: N-Acyl-L-homoserine lactone EcoCyc common name: an N-acyl-L-homoserine lactone. N-acyl-L-homoserine lactones are as family of compounds that function as bacterial autoinducers, facilitating cell-cell communication. This process, termed quorum sensing, allows bacterial populations to coordinate gene expression as a function of cell density. Many processes benefit from community cooperation, including bioluminescence, virulence factor expression, antibiotic production and biofilm development. Many types of autoinducers exist, including Acyl-homoserine-lactones "N-acylhomoserine lactones" (AHLs), Gamma-Butyrolactones "γ-butyrolactones", MMFs "methylenomycin furans", CPD-19905, Butenolides "γ-butenolides", and Dialkylresorcinols dialkylresorcinols (DARs).
