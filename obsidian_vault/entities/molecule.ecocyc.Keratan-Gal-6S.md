## Enriched Summary

KERATAN-6-SULFATE "Keratan sulfate" is a glycosaminoglycan found mostly in the cornea, cartilage, and bone. It is a linear polymer that consists of a repeating disaccharide unit. The basic repeating disaccharide unit consists of GALACTOSE and N-ACETYL-BETA-D-GLUCOSAMINE, either of which can be sulfated at carbon position 6.
