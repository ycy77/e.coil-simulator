## Enriched Summary

When translation is not under stress, an RNA hairpin spanning the toiL uORF and the topAI ribosome-binding site masks the topAI RBS, blocking translation initiation and thereby favoring premature Rho-dependent transcription termination within the topAI coding region . In this repressed state, toiL is likely translated efficiently, but ribosomes moving through toiL only briefly unwind the hairpin. In contrast, when translation stress is triggered-particularly by ribosome-targeting antibiotics-ribosomes stall within toiL, which disrupts the inhibitory hairpin, allows topAI translation to proceed, and consequently suppresses premature Rho-dependent termination within topAI . ToiL is conserved across a variety of Enterobacteriaceae species .
