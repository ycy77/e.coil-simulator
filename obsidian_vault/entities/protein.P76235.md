## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: May play a role in the adaptation to sustained nitrogen starvation. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:26621053}. YeaH plays a role in the adaptation to sustained nitrogen starvation . The CPLX0-1461-dependent RYEB-RNA was found to downregulate the translation of G6969-MONOMER and YeaH under long term (24 hrs) N-starved conditions. Recovery from N-starvation has a shorter lag time in ΔyeaG, ΔyeaH and ΔyeaGΔyeaH mutants, whereas a longer lag time was observed in ΔsdsR mutants; thus, SdsR translational downregulation of YeaG and YeaH allows for optimal recovery from N-starvation .
