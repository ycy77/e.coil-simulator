## Enriched Summary

12-Hydroxyjasmonates "Tuberonic acid" (12-hydroxyjasmonate) was first described from potato and Solanaceous species for its tuber-inducing activity, hence its trivial name . However, it was later detected in many plant species, including TAX-3702, and is often associated with would response . At least two different stereoisomers, CPD-11260 and CPD-11253, exist in TAX-3702, where the former appears to be more abundant . The biological activity of tuberonic acid may differ between species.
