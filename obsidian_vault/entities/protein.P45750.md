## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Required for the use of extracellular DNA as a nutrient. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16707682}. The yrfA gene product is required for utilization of DNA as the sole source of carbon and energy; yrfA is homologous to genes involved in natural competence and genetic transformation in other bacteria . YrfA is predicted to be located in the inner membrane . A yrfA null mutant has a normal growth phenotype in complex media, but has a stationary phase competition-defective (SPCD) phenotype in co-culture with the wild-type strain. A yrfA null mutant can not utilize DNA as the sole source of carbon and energy . yrfA is poorly expressed .
