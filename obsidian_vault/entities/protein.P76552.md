## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Probably involved in the diffusion of protonated ethanolamine (EA) into the cell at low pH. At low pH most EA is protonated, and this permease becomes necessary. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P41796}. Sequence analysis has identified EutH as a membrane protein with 11 transmembrane helices . A possible ethanolamine transport function for EutH has been suggested . Although it was initially found that a eutH mutant in Salmonella enterica is not defective for ethanolamine utilization , further studies showed a pH-dependent effect, and it is now thought that EutH facilitates diffusion of protonated ethanolamine across the cytoplasmic membrane .
