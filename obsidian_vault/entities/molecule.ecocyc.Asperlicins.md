## Enriched Summary

The peptidyl alkaloids of the Asperlicins asperlicin family are valued as therapeutics with promising potential due to their ability to act as potent competitive inhibitors of the gallbladder peptide hormone cholycystokinin (CKK), which is involved in fat and protein digestion .
