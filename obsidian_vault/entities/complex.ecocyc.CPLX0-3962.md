## Enriched Summary

Assembly and biogenesis of the ribosome and the two subunits, the small 30S and large 50S ribosomal subunits, in E. coli have been ongoing for more than 50 years. The large subunit assembly was initially determined in vitro by examining the direct interactions of isolated proteins and purified RNAs that interact with the 5S and 23S RNAs then by identifying and mapping proteins involved with assembling the full 50S subunit . CryoEM structural analyses have provided more details on the in vivo assembly of the large ribosomal subunit and intermediate stages .
