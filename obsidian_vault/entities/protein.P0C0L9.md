## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: May function as iron donor in the assembly of iron-sulfur clusters. IscX appears to act as a regulator of iron-sulfur cluster assembly. IscX physically interacts with CPLX0-248 IscS and G7324-MONOMER IscU . It was suggested that IscX functions as a molecular adaptor that is able to modulate the function of IscS in iron-sulfur cluster formation . IscX addition to the IscU-IscS binary complex reduces the cysteine desulfurase activity of the ternary complex and inhibits assembly of Fe-S clusters on IscU in vitro . In vivo, IscX has a positive effect on Fe-S cluster biogenesis . The residues involved in binding Fe2+ and Fe3+ are also involved in the interaction of IscX with IscS . IscX, EG11653-MONOMER CyaY, and FERREDOXIN-MONOMER Fdx compete for overlapping binding sites on IscS . The presence of Fe2+ strengthens the interaction between IscX and IscU . IscX may be involved in the assembly of the [2Fe-2S] cluster of Fdx . A crystal structure of IscX has been solved at 1.75 Å resolution , and a solution structure is available . An iscX mutant exhibits no obvious phenotype, compared to wild type . Only a competitive growth experiment revealed that a ΔiscX mutant has a growth disadvantage compared to wild type .
