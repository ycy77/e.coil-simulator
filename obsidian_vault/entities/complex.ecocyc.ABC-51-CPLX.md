## Enriched Summary

YdcS, YdcT, YdcU and YdcV are the predicted components of an uncharacterized ABC transporter . YdcS is the putative periplasmic binding component, YdcT is the putative ATP binding component, YdcU and YdcV are the putative membrane spanning components. ydcSTUV forms an operon with patD (encoding CPLX0-3641); transcription is initiated by σs containing RNA polymerase and induced under nitrogen limited growth . YdcSTUV has been annotated as a putative putrescine/spermidine transporter in the literature (see for example ; an in-frame deletion of ydcSTUV has no effect on putrescine utilization . YdcS and YdcV have been implicated in the transport of double strand DNA during natural and/or chemical transformation .
