## Enriched Summary

Retinol is one of the animal forms of vitamin A. It is convertible to other forms of vitamin A, and the Retinyl-Esters "retinyl ester" derivative of the alcohol serves as the storage form of the vitamin in animals. Retinol is produced in the body from the hydrolysis of Retinyl-Esters "retinyl esters", and from the reduction of RETINAL. Retinol is one of the animal forms of vitamin A.
