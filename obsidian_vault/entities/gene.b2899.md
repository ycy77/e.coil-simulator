## Enriched Summary

trhA (gene.b2899) is a gene entity. It encodes yqfA (protein.P67153). Encoded protein function: UPF0073 inner membrane protein YqfA EcoCyc product frame: G7512-MONOMER. EcoCyc synonyms: yqfA. Genomic coordinates: 3042489-3043148. EcoCyc protein note: TrhA (formerly YqfA) modulates the ability to maintain optimal cell membrane energetics possibly via an effect on unsaturated fatty acid metabolism. A ΔtrhA mutant shows pleiotropic physiological changes consistent with dysregulated membrane energetics; mechanism not known . Deletion of trhA and site-specific mutations of trhA alter cell morphology; ΔtrhA cells contain fewer long unsaturarated fatty acids and an increased abundance of saturated fatty acids compared to wild type; TrhA modulates the relative abundance of membrane unsaturated fatty acids . TrhA is related to the progestin and AdipoQ receptor (PAQR) family of proteins which function to maintain membrane fluidity and membrane fatty acid composition in eukaryotes . trhA is part of the FabR regulon; trhA is coregulated with genes of unsaturated fatty acid synthesis . trhA is upregulated under multiple stress conditions (heat, cold, oxidative stress, nitrosative stress, and antibiotic treatment) .
