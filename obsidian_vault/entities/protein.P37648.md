## Enriched Summary

Protein YhjJ bipP encodes a periplasmic protein that regulates the EG10760-MONOMER Prc-EG12371-MONOMER Nlp proteolytic system; BipP controls the level of peptidoglycan-cleaving enzymes G7147-MONOMER MepS, G6892-MONOMER MepH and EG10246-MONOMER MltD, by negatively regulating the NlpI-Prc system in response to peptidoglycan hydrolase activity; BipP controls the NlpI-Prc system through its interaction with NlpI; BipP binds to NlpI and inhibits substrate recognition through steric hindrance . BipP contains a metallopeptidase M16 domain but lacks critical metal-binding residues . Overexpression of yhjJ from an ASKA plasmid leads to a more than 3-fold increase in the MIC of hydrogen peroxide . bipP: brake on NlpI-Prc-promoted proteolysis .
