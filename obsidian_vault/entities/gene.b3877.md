yihP (gene.b3877) is a gene entity. It encodes yihP (protein.P32137). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: Could be involved in the export of 2,3-dihydroxypropane-1-sulfonate (DHPS). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:24463506}. EcoCyc product frame: YIHP-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 4065809-4067194. EcoCyc protein note: yihP is part of a 10 gene cluster which is conserved across E. coli species and has been implicated in sulfoquinovose catabolism. The YihP protein may function to export 2-3-dihydroxypropane-1-sulfonate (an end-product of sulfoquinovose catabolism) but it has not been experimentally characterised . The YihP protein is a member of the glycoside-pentoside-hexuronide (GPH) cation symporter family of transporters .
