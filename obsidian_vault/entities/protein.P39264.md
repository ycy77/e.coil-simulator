## Enriched Summary

Fimbrin-like protein FimI The fimI gene is located in the fim gene cluster of Escherichia coli and has been shown to encode a 16.4 kDa noncytoplasmic protein product. Deletion mutations in fimI produced a piliation-negative phenotype, however the function of the FimI protein in pilus biosynthesis has not been determined . E. coli K-12 contains 12 chaperone-usher fimbrial operons, including fimAICDFGH; a fimbria-lacking mutant - strain WQM026 - created by deleting all 12 operons has been characterised .
