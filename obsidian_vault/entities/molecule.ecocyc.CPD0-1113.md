## Enriched Summary

Fosfomycin is a broad-spectrum antibiotic produced by certain Streptomyces species. There are several different stereochemical representations of this compound in the literature . The one shown here is according to . Fosfomycin is a mimetic of PHOSPHO-ENOL-PYRUVATE, and irreversibly binds to the catalytic cysteine residue of EC-2.5.1.7 (G-11844), blocking peptidoglycan biosynthesis in Gram-negative bacteria.
