## Enriched Summary

Sphingoids (sphingoid bases, or long chain bases) are building blocks of SPHINGOLIPIDS sphingolipids. They are amino alcohols, composed predominantly (but not exclusively) of 18 carbon atoms. They are characterized by the presence of a hydroxyl group at C1 (and often also at C3), and an amine group at C2. The commonly known sphingoids include SPHINGOSINE, D-erythro-Shinganines sphinganine (dihydrosphingosine), and Phytosphingosines phytosphingosine (4-hydroxysphinganine).
