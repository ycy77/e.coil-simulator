Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: Involved in iron-sulfur (Fe-S) cluster assembly (PubMed:16603772, PubMed:19305405, PubMed:21799759, PubMed:25430730). May act as a regulator of Fe-S biogenesis (PubMed:19305405, PubMed:21799759, PubMed:25430730).
