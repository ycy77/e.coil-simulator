## Enriched Summary

Glycoconjugate is the general term used for carbohydrates that are covalently linked with other chemical species such as proteins, peptides, lipids and saccharides. Examples for glycoconjugates include Glycoconjugates glycoproteins, glycopeptides, Peptidoglycans peptidoglycans, Glycolipids glycolipids, Glycosides glycosides and Lipopolysaccharides lipopolysaccharides. Glycoconjugates are often involved in cell-cell interactions, cell-cell recognition, cell-matrix interactions, and detoxification processes.
