## Enriched Summary

DIHYDRO-SPHINGOSINE Sphinganines can have C14, C16, C18 or C20 skeletons. They also exist as different stereoisomers. The D-erythro-Shinganines "D-erythro isomers" are the biologically relevant forms , but the L-threo isomers may have anticancer properties .
