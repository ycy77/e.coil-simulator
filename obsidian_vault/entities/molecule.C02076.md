KEGG compound: Sedoheptulose; D-altro-Heptulose; D-Sedoheptulose; Volemulose EcoCyc common name: D-sedoheptulose. SEDOHEPTULOSE is a relatively unknown metabolite compared with its phosphorylated form, D-SEDOHEPTULOSE-7-P, which is an intermediate molecule of primary glucose metabolism. The natural existence of SEDOHEPTULOSE, a keto-heptose, was first reported in plants and was subsequently identified in human urine , blood , within mouse cells and in fungi .
