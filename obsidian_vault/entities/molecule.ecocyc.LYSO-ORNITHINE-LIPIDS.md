## Enriched Summary

A lyso-ornithine lipid contains a 3-hydroxy fatty acyl group attached in an amide linkage to the α-amino group of L-ORNITHINE (reviewed in ). See also ORNITHINE-LIPIDS. The configuration of the asymetric carbon of the 3-hydroxy fatty acids of several bacterial ornithine lipids was detrmined to be D (R) .
