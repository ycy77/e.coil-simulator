## Enriched Summary

CPD-12146 "Copper-complexed cysteinyltyrosyl radical" is a cofactor of MONOMER-15357 (GALACTOSE-OXIDASE-RXN "EC 1.1.3.9"). This cofactor involves a thioether bond between an TYR and an CYS in the enzyme's polypeptide chain, and an CU+2 atom. The active site also contains a free radical on the tyrosine . The copper site, which is not shown in the structure below, has five ligands in square pyramidal coordination. Tyr 272, His 496, His 581 and an acetate ion form an almost perfect square around the copper ion. The fifth ligand, which is axial, is Tyr 495 (amino acid numbering is bassed on the enzyme from TAX-5132) .
