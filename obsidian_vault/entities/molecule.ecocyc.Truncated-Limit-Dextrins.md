## Enriched Summary

Truncated-Limit-Dextrins "An α-limit dextrin with short branches" is formed when CPD0-971 is subjected to the ENZRXN-14616 activity of the YPR184W-MONOMER. The enzyme shortens the branching chains, leaving only single glucose residues connected by an α-1,6 bond to the main (α-1,4)-glucan chain.
