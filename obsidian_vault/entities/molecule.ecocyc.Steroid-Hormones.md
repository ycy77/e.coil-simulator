## Enriched Summary

Steroid hormones are steroids that act as hormones. Steroid hormones are divided into five groups based on the receptors to which they bind: Glucocorticoids glucocorticoids, Mineralocorticoids mineralocorticoids, Androgens androgens, Steroidal-Estrogens "steroidal estrogens", and Progestogens progestogens. Certain vitamin D derivatives (e.g. CALCITRIOL) are sometimes considered a sixth steroid hormone group. The natural steroid hormones of mammals are generally synthesized from cholesterol in the gonads and adrenal glands. Steroid hormones are steroids that act as hormones.
