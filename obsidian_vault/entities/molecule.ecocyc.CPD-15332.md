## Enriched Summary

Free uric acid is not found in urine. Instead, it is present as CPD-15041 and quadrurate salts . Excess accumulation of uric acid in the blood can lead to a type of arthritis known as gout, during which needle-like crystals of uric acid salts precipitate in joints, capillaries, skin, and other tissues.
