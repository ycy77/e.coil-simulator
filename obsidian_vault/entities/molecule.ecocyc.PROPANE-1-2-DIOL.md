## Enriched Summary

1,2-Propanediol (1,2-PDO) occurs in two stereoisomers - PROPANE-1-2-DIOL and CPD-8891. Both forms are produced by certain organisms. For example, during the catabolism of 6-deoxyhexoses or during the fermentation of glucose via methylglyoxal . 1,2-PDO is a bulk industrial chemical with a global demand of around 1.36 million tons/year . It is widely used in the production of unsaturated polyester resin and as a nontoxic replacement of ethylene glycol in deicer and antifreeze products.
