## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Benzene Benzene is a highly toxic industrial compound with a broad range of industrial uses. It is one of the top 20 production volume chemicals in the United States, with an annual production of almost 9 million metric tons. In addition to being a component of petroleum-based fuels, benzene is often used as a raw material for the manufacture of other chemicals, rubbers, lubricants, dyes, detergents, drugs, and pesticides. Benzene is among the most prevalent organic contaminants in groundwater and is a major concern due to its toxicity and relatively high solubility .
