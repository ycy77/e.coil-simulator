## Enriched Summary

Arabinogalactan Arabinogalactans are biopolymers consisting of arabinose and galactose residues. Two main classes of arabinogalactans are found in nature: Plant-Arabinogalactans "plant arabinogalactan" and Mycobacterial-Arabinogalactans "microbial arabinogalactan". Plant-Arabinogalactans "Plant arabinogalactan" is a major constituent of many gums, and a major component of the Hemicelluloses of some plants. Mycobacterial-Arabinogalactans "Microbial arabinogalactan" is a major structural component of the mycobacterial cell wall.
