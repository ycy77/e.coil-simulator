## Enriched Summary

Sulfur-Carrier-Proteins-TusE TusE functions as a sulfur mediator during synthesis of 2-thiouridine of the modified wobble base mnm5s2U in tRNA. TusE directly interacts with both the TusBCD complex and the MnmA-tRNA complex, but not with tRNA itself . 2-thiouridine formation in vitro is greatly enhanced in the presence of TusE. TusE is labeled by 35S sulfur during 2-thiouridine formation in vitro . A tusE deletion mutant lacks mnm5s2U, accumulates mnm5U and has a growth defect. The cysteine residue C108 is required for sulfur transfer .
