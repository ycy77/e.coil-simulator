## Enriched Summary

Unlike most other cellular proteins, synthesis of YfbU is increased after induction of the toxin EG11249-MONOMER MazF . yfbU mRNA is transcribed as a leaderless mRNA. MazF cleavage of the 16S rRNA removes its anti-Shine-Dalgarno sequence, thereby generating a specialized "stress-ribosome" that preferentially translates leaderless mRNAs . A crystal structure of YfbU has been solved at 2.5 Å resolution. 24 subunits of YfbU form a particle with an ~55 Å diameter empty cavity . Under growth conditions causing DNA damage, most of the population of a yfbU mutant survives, phenocopying a ΔmazEF mutant .
