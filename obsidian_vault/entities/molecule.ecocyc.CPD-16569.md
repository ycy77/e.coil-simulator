## Enriched Summary

CPD-16569 Kojibiose is a disaccharide that was initially characterized from koji extract [an extract produced from rice by the application of TAX-5062 (koji in Japanese) spores, an early step in the production of the Japanese liquor Sho-chu] , and later from hydrol , honey and dextrans . Kojibiose is also found as a constituent of polysaccharides and the sugar chains of glycoproteins . It can be produced from glucose by caramelization . The structure, initially thought to be 3-O-α-D-glucopyranosyl-D-glucose, was shown to be 2-O-α-D-glucopyranosyl-D-glucose a few years later . Crystalization was achieved in 1961 .
