## Enriched Summary

Oxyvinylglycines are non-proteinogenic amino acids that are produced by certain bacteria. They irreversibly inhibit enzymes that require PYRIDOXAL_PHOSPHATE as a co-factor and thus have multiple targets in bacteria, animals, and plants . One such plant target is the ethylene biosynthesis enzyme EC-4.4.1.14, which is inhibited by CPD-13353 (AVG) and by CPD-20103 . AVG is commercially available under the name of ReTain and is widely used for the regulation of fruit set in orchard crops. Another member of the oxyvinylglycines, CPD-20100 (FVG), is a natural herbicide that blocks the germination of a large variety of grassy weed species .
