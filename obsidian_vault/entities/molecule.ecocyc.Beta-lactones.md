## Enriched Summary

A Lactones "lactone" is a cyclic ester, the product of condensation of an alcohol group and a carboxylate group within the same molecule. It is characterized by a closed ring consisting of two or more carbon atoms and a single oxygen atom, with a ketone group in one of the carbons adjacent to the ring oxygen. There are several types of lactones - Beta-lactones contain 3 carbons in the ring, Gamma-lactones contains 4, Delta-lactones contains 5, and Epsilon-lactones contains 6 carbons.
