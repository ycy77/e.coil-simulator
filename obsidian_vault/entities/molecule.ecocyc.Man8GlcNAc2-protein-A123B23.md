## Enriched Summary

This is one of three isomers of Man8GlcNAc2 chains that are found in N glycans. The nomenclature used to identify high mannose glycans has not been standardized. Several earlier systems were insufficient to describe all possible isomers . The nomenclature used here is that proposed by Prien et al , which can represent all processing isomers. According to this nomenclature, the other two Man8GlcNAc2 isomers are named Man8-GlcNAc2-protein-A123B12 "isomer 8A1,2,3B1,2" and Man8GlcNAc2-protein-A123B13 "isomer 8A1,2,3B1,3" .
