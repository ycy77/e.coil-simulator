Putative metabolite transport protein YdjK In the Transporter Classification Database, YdjK is an uncharacterised member of the Sugar Porter (SP) Family with the Major Facilitator Superfamily (MFS) . YdjK is implicated in maintaining antibiotic tolerance under starvation; ydjK is upregulated during nutrient starvation and the starvation-induced ampicillin tolerance levels of a ydjK knockout strain are significantly reduced over a 6 day period .
