## Enriched Summary

CPD-12193 Ricinoleate is an unsaturated ω-9 fatty acid (indicating the double bond is located 9 carbons from the methyl end of the molecule). It is a major component of the seed oil obtained from TAX-3988 (Castor bean) seeds. CPD-12193 Ricinoleate is also a major component of he sclerotium (a compact mass of hardened fungal mycelium containing food reserves) produced by the ergot fungus (TAX-5111). Like other fatty acids, CPD-12193 is rarely found in its free form. It is usually found either as CPD-15364 or CPD-17396 "incorporated into a lipid".
