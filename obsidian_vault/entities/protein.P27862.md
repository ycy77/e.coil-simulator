## Enriched Summary

IMPACT family member YigZ yigZ is a widely conserved gene; its mammalian homolog is the imprinted (paternally expressed) mouse gene impact . A crystal structure of YigZ has been solved at 2.8 Å resolution. The C-terminal domain shows weak structural similarity to elongation factors and suggests that the protein may bind nucleic acids . In a large genetic interaction screen, yigZ showed genetic interactions with a number of proteins involved in translation and ribosome biogenesis. A ΔyigZ mutant shows increased levels of stop codon readthrough . In a large-scale TraDIS screen, fewer yigZ mutants were found in biofilm conditions compared to planktonic growth . The yigZ ortholog from commensal E. coli M13 strain has two amino acid differences from strain MG1655 at residues 25 and 146. When this ortholog is overexpressed in strain MG1655, the colonization in mice increased substantially indicating the allelic variation in YigZ that confers colonization capacity. YigZ is found throughout major gut colonizing microbes and is considered a conserved gut colonization factor .
