## Enriched Summary

The Lyso-Form-Lipoprotein-Cysteine "lyso form" of Lipoproteins lipoproteins, which differs from the a-mature-triacylated-lipoprotein "lipoproteins that have been observed in Gram-negative bacteria" by missing the acyl moiety at the sn-2 position of the glyceryl residue, has been observed in several low GC Gram-positive bacteria, including TAX-1351, TAX-1396, TAX-1305, and TAX-1585 .
