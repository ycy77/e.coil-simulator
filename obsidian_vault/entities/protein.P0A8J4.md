## Enriched Summary

UPF0250 protein YbeD YbeD is involved in the response to high-temperature stress. A ΔybeD strain grows more slowly and to a lower cell density than wild type, while overexpression of ybeD enhances growth at 46°C and alleviates the growth phenotypes of several chaperone mutants . A solution structure of YbeD shows structural similarity to small-molecule binding ACT domains . In E. coli BL21(DE3), ybeD expression increases significantly after a shift from 37°C to 42°C or 46°C .
