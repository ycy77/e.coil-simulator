## Enriched Summary

Auxins are a class of plant hormones, and were the first of the major plant hormones to be discovered. The distribution of auxins within the plant is a key factor that influences growth, reaction to the environment, and organ development. The four naturally occurring auxins are INDOLE_ACETATE_AUXIN (IAA), CPD-14644, PHENYLACETATE and CPD-10507; IAA is by far the most important auxin and generates the majority of auxin effects in intact plants Auxins are a class of plant hormones, and were the first of the major plant hormones to be discovered.
