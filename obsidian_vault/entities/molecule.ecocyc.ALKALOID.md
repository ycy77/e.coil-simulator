## Enriched Summary

Alkaloids are naturally occurring heterocyclic compounds that contain nitrogen and an alkaline pH (hence the name). Alkaloids are secondary metabolites, and have a marked physiological action on animal physiology. They are produced by a large variety of organisms, including bacteria, fungi, plants, and animals.
