## Enriched Summary

Chondroitin is a naturally occurring heteropolysaccharide belonging to the family of glycosaminoglycans. It is a nonsulfated derivative of Chondroitin-sulfates, consisting of a β-D-glucuronyl-(1,3)-N-acetyl-β-D-galactosamine disaccharide repeating unit connected through a β(1,4) glycosidic linkages. Chondroitin is widely used as a therapeutic material for the prevention or alleviation of symptoms of diseases such as rheumatoid arthritis, and in food supplements.
