## Enriched Summary

Ankyrin repeat protein A (Ankyrin-like regulatory protein) ArpA has ankyrin-like repeats . A meta-analysis of E. coli K-12 MG1655 transcriptomic data sets in response to multiple stressors found 15 uncharacterized or partially characterized genes (yqfA, yhfG, yhhA, ybgS, arpA, yqaE, yfdY, yaiY, yebE, ydiE, yjcB, yiiX, ycjF, yihF, yidX) that were upregulated under all five stressors: heat, cold, oxidative stress, nitrosative stress, and antibiotic treatment, whereas 3 uncharacterized or partially characterized genes (ymfI, ydiJ, yedE) that were downregulated under all but nitrosative stress . Lack of arpA is strongly linked to neonatal meningitis caused by some pathogenic E. coli strains .
