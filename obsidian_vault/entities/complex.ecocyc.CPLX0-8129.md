## Enriched Summary

YbcN is encoded by the cryptic lambdoid prophage DLP12 and is a distantly related member of the Orf phage protein family. The protein binds to single-stranded DNA as well as to double-stranded DNA containing base mismatches and bubbles . YbcN interacts with the C terminus of EG10976-MONOMER . YbcN binds to double-stranded DNA with a G:T mismatch and flips the mismatched base out of the DNA duplex into an extrahelical conformation. It may thus be involved in DNA mismatch repair . ybcN mutants are weak suppressors of the growth defect of a ΔyciM mutant .
