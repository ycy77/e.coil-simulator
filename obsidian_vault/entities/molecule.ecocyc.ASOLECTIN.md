## Enriched Summary

Asolectin is a mixture of phospholipids obtained commercially from soybeans. It comprises roughly equal proportions of PHOSPHATIDYLCHOLINE phosphatidylcholine, L-1-PHOSPHATIDYL-ETHANOLAMINE phosphatidylethanolamine and Phosphatidylinositols phosphatidylinositol, along with minor amounts of other phospholipids and polar lipids .
