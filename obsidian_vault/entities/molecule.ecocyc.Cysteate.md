## Enriched Summary

Cysteate serves as a sole source of carbon and energy for the aerobic growth of bacteria , as an electron acceptor for several sulfate-reducing bacteria , as an electron donor for some nitrate-reducing bacteria , and as the substrate for a fermentation in a sulfate-reducing bacterium .
