## Enriched Summary

Aurachin alkaloids were originally isolated from the myxobacterium TAX-675526 in the 1980s . They are potent inhibitors of mitochondrial respiration through blockage of NADH oxidation, presumably because of their significant structural similarity to Menaquinones menaquinone . CPD-7733 "Aurachin C" is a particularly effective inhibitor of the two terminal oxidases of TAX-562, CYT-O-UBIOX-CPLX "cytochromes bo" and CYT-D-UBIOX-CPLX "cytochrome bd", whereas CPD-15909 displays a selectivity for inhibition of cytochrome bd . Beside TAX-40, only one other producer species has been discovered - the Gram-positive bacterium TAX-1833, which produces CPD-15906 . Despite the fact that these two organisms are phylogenetically unrelated, the genes that encode the biosyntheric enzymes share some similarity . ## Annotation

Aurachin alkaloids were originally isolated from the myxobacterium TAX-675526 in the 1980s . They are potent inhibitors of mitochondrial respiration through blockage of NADH oxidation, presumably because of their significant structural similarity to Menaquinones menaquinone . CPD-7733 "Aurachin C" is a particularly effective inhibitor of the two terminal oxidases of TAX-562, CYT-O-UBIOX-CPLX "cytochromes bo" and CYT-D-UBIOX-CPLX "cytochrome bd", whereas CPD-15909 displays a selectivity for inhibition of cytochrome bd . Beside TAX-40, only one other producer species has been discovered - the Gram-positive bacterium TAX-1833, which produces CPD-15906 . Despite the fact that these two organisms are phylogenetically unrelated, the genes that encode the biosyntheric enzymes share some similarity .
