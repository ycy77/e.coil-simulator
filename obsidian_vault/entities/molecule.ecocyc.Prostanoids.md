## Enriched Summary

The term prostanoid is used to describe a subclass of Icosanoids icosanoids that includes the Prostaglandins prostaglandins and the Thromboxanes thromboxanes. These lipid compounds are derived from the ω-6 essential fatty acid ARACHIDONIC_ACID. Every prostanoid contains 20 carbon atoms with an internal carbon ring structure.
