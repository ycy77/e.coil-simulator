## Enriched Summary

β-1,4-Mannans are major constitutents of hemicelluloses in plant tissue and are polymers composed of β(1→4)-linked mannose residues. Some contain galactopyranosyl side chains (see Galactomannans). Slightly galactosylated mannans (4% galactose), considered as linear β(1→4)-D-mannans, have been isolated from the seed endosperm of vegetable ivory nut (TAX-115507) and date (TAX-42345) . Note that there are several types of mannans. The proteins in yeast cell walls contain particularly complex N-glycans referred to as α-mannans in which the core N-glycan is extended by ~200 α-1,6-mannose units that are also decorated with α-linked mannose side chains .
