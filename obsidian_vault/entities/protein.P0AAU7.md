Uncharacterized protein YbfE Levels of ybfE mRNA are increased by exposure to the DNA damaging agent mitomycin C; the increase is dependent on LexA. A putative LexA binding site with a very high "heterology index" (HI) score has been identified upstream of the YbfE open reading frame .
