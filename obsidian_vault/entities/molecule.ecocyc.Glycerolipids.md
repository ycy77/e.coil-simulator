## Enriched Summary

Glycerolipids are lipids that contain a GLYCEROL backbone to which at least one fatty acyl group is esterified. If a phosphate group is ester-linked to the terminal carbon of the GLYCEROL backbone, the compound is classified as Phosphoglycerides.
