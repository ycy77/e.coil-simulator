## Enriched Summary

yjjB (gene.b4363) is a gene entity. It encodes yjjB (protein.P0ADD2). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: Involved in succinate export with YjjP. Both proteins are required for export (PubMed:28673128). Contributes to succinate production under both aerobic and anaerobic conditions (PubMed:28673128). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28673128}. EcoCyc product frame: EG11215-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 4601624-4602097. EcoCyc protein note: yjjB and yjjP are implicated in succinate export. Deletion of these genes in an E. coli K-12 strain that has been engineered for anaerobic succinate production decreases succinate production significantly; heterologous expression of these genes in an engineered strain of Pantoea ananatis enhanced succinate production under aerobic conditions; heterologous expression of these genes in an engineered strain of Corynebacterium glutamicum restored succinate production under anaerobic conditions . YjjB contains 4 predicted transmembrane regions, YjjP contains 5 predicted transmembrane regions and together these proteins may constitute a 'split-type' ThrE family transporter . Homologs of YjjP and YjjB are present in other gram-negative succinate producing species such as Enterobacter aerogenes, Actinobacillus succinogenes and Mannheimia succiniciproducens .
