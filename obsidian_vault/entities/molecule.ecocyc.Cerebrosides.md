## Enriched Summary

Cerebrosides consist of Ceramides with a single sugar residue at the 1-hydroxyl moiety. The sugar residue can be either glucose or galactose, and the two major types are thus named Glucosyl-ceramides glucocerebrosides and D-galactosylceramide galactocerebrosides.
