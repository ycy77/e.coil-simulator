## Enriched Summary

This compound is present in the flavedo (epicarp) of oranges (TAX-2711) fed with radiolabeled INDOLE_ACETATE_AUXIN, DIOXINDOLE-3-ACETATE or 2-OXINDOLE-3-ACETIC-ACID . There is strong evidence that the hexose present in orange is glucose (see: CPD-9642) but, galactose, mannose, and lactose cannot be conclusively excluded .
