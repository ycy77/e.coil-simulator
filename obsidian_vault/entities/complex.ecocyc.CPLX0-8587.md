## Enriched Summary

UspD is one of six universal stress proteins in E. coli. UspC, UspD, and UspE play nonredundant and possibly sequential roles in a pathway involved in resistance to UV irradiation . Mutation of the uspC, uspD, or uspE gene causes decreased resistance to UV irradiation . Deletion of uspD leads to decreased resistance to superoxide and to streptonigrin . Expression of uspD is upregulated during starvation and certain other stresses and requires ppGpp, but not σS . uspD is part of the σE and YdeO regulons.
