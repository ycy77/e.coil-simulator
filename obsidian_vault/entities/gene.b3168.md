## Enriched Summary

infB (gene.b3168) is a gene entity. It encodes infB (protein.P0A705). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: One of the essential components for the initiation of protein synthesis. May protect N-formylmethionyl-tRNA(fMet) from spontaneous hydrolysis. Promotes N-formylmethionyl-tRNA(fMet) binding to the 30S pre-initiation complex (PIC) (PubMed:1764105, PubMed:20224578). Also involved in the hydrolysis of GTP during the formation of the 70S ribosomal complex. Upon addition of the 50S ribosomal subunit, IF-1, IF-2 and IF-3 are released leaving the mature 70S translation initiation complex. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1764105, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20224578, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22562136, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2444251}. EcoCyc product frame: MONOMER0-4539. EcoCyc synonyms: ssyG. Genomic coordinates: 3313342-3316014. EcoCyc protein note: For curated information on translation initiation factor IF-2 and its isoforms (generated by alternative translation initiation), please see EG10505-MONOMER. EcoCyc protein note: IF2 is one of three translation initiation factors in E. coli ; together with IF1, it ensures the correct binding of fMet-tRNAfMet in the ribosomal P site . IF2 together with the 50S subunit stabilizes the mRNA in the correct reading frame in the late initiation complex and stabilizes the assembled 70S complex...
