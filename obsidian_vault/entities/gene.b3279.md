## Enriched Summary

yrdA (gene.b3279) is a gene entity. It encodes yrdA (protein.P0A9W9). Encoded protein function: Protein YrdA EcoCyc product frame: G7696-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 3429236-3429790. EcoCyc protein note: YrdA belongs to the γ class of carbonic anhydrases (γ-CA). During cloning, a point mutation, Arg6His, and a C-terminal His tail were introduced; when purified, this protein only has little detectable carbonic anhydrase activity . An N-terminally His-tagged recombinant γ-CA based on accession WP_009008373.1, which contains an additional 72 N-terminal amino acids and is identical to YrdA in its C-terminal 184 amino acids, is able to catalyze hydration of CO2 with a kcat of 5.7 x 105 s-1 . Crystal structures of YrdA have been solved; the protein forms a homotrimer in the crystal . The expression of YrdA protein is induced by the addition of spermine to the growth media at pH 8.5 . Using a method that distinguishes N-phosphorylation from O-phosphorylation, N-phosphorylation was detected in vitro . The Keio collection yrdA mutant exhibits a 'low level' of resistance to lithium exposure .
