## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Inositol 1-phosphate; myo-Inositol 1-phosphate; 1D-myo-Inositol 1-phosphate; D-myo-Inositol 1-phosphate; 1D-myo-Inositol 1-monophosphate EcoCyc common name: 1D-myo-inositol 1-monophosphate. There are two isomers of myo-inositol-1-phosphate: 1-L-MYO-INOSITOL-1-P "1L-myo-inositol 1-phosphate" is the compound formed from glucose 6-phosphate in the biosynthesis of inositol. D-MYO-INOSITOL-1-MONOPHOSPHATE is the constituent of phospholipids and inositol polyphosphates. Please note that 1L-myo-inositol 1-phosphate is also called 1D-myo-inositol 3-phosphate. Additional information on the nomenclature of myo-inositol compounds can be found in the document Numbering of atoms in myo-inositol, by the Nomenclature Committee of the International Union of Biochemistry (NC-IUB).
