## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Absolutely required for transposition of IS1. insA-7 is a fragment of the IS1 insertion sequence coding for the transcriptional repressor protein InsA . Normally, IS1 codes for three proteins, but the IS1 sequence that codes for insA-7 contains a nonsense mutation that prevents the translation of the InsB and InsAB' proteins .
