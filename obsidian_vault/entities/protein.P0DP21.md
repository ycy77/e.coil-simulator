## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: This pseudogene is the N-terminal fragment of low activity DNA endonuclease RpnD which probably yields 3'-hydroxyl ends (By similarity). The intact protein can be seen in this entry (AC B7NGZ6). Expression of the repaired protein increases the frequency of recA-independent recombination, but also decreases viability probably via DNA damage; in a RecA strain expression has no effect on viability but does induce the SOS repair response (PubMed:26162088, PubMed:26618540). May play a role in horizontal gene transfer (PubMed:26162088, PubMed:26618540, PubMed:28096446). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P31667, ECO:0000269|PubMed:26162088, ECO:0000269|PubMed:26618540, ECO:0000269|PubMed:28096446}. RpnD (YjiP/YjiQ) is one of five proteins in E. coli that belong to the "transposase_31" family , which is distantly related to the PD-(D/E)XK nuclease superfamily . It was previously noted that YjiP/Q has similarity to RpnC, which is encoded within the panBCD gene cluster . YjiP is encoded near the so-called immigration control region (ICR), which is a variable genomic island that contains a number of restriction-modification systems .
