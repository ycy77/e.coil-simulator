## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized protein YmcE YmcE was mistakenly thought to be a suppressor of fabA6, a temperature-sensitive unsaturated fatty acid auxotroph ; in fact, the suppressor localizes to the gnsA gene, which is located immediately downstream of ymcE . YmcE is also predicted to be the antitoxin of a proposed GnsA-YmcE toxin-antitoxin system . YmcE is predicted to be a bitopic inner membrane protein . Expression of ymcE is increased by cold shock . Overexpression of ymcE increases the tolerance of E. coli to n-butanol .
