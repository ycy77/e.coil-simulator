## Enriched Summary

CREPENYNATE Crepenynate (crepenynic acid) is a fatty acid with an 18-carbon chain containing a cis double bond at position 9 and a triple bond at position 12. Like other fatty acids, CREPENYNATE is rarely found in its free form. It is usually found Glycerolipid-crepenynate "incorporated into a lipid". CREPENYNATE Crepenynate makes up to 70% of the fatty acids in triacylglycerol from the seed oil of the plant TAX-72610 .
