## Enriched Summary

This class contains DL-CARNITINE molecules esterified at the C3 hydroxyl to a wide range of acyl groups, including acetyl, or short, medium, or long chain fatty acyl groups . The general role of acylcarnitines is to transport acyl-groups (organic acids and fatty acids) from the cytoplasm into the mitochondria so that they can be broken down to produce energy.
