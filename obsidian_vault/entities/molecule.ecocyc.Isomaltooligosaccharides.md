## Enriched Summary

Isoaltooligosaccharides are glucooligosaccharide derived from D-glucose monomers linked via α-1,6 bonds as in Isomaltose. More specifically, the term is applied to linear oligosaccharides composed of more than two and less than ten units of glucose. The corresponding α-1,4 glucooligosaccharides are called Malto-Oligosaccharides maltooligosaccharides.
