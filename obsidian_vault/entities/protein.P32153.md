## Enriched Summary

Putative aminopeptidase FrvX (EC 3.4.11.-) frvX is a member of the frvABXR operon which encodes a putative fructose-specific PEP-dependent sugar phosphotransferase system . frvX is predicted to encode a water soluble protein of unknown function . Experiments with plasmid-borne lux-promoter fusions show frvX is upregulated during growth with sulfometuron methyl, which inhibits the first enzyme of valine and isoleucine biosynthesis . frvX was also shown to be upregulated during growth with nalidixic acid .
