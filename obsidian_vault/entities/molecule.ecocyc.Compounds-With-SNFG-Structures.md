## Enriched Summary

This class holds compounds that may possess an SNFG structure. Symbol Nomenclature for Glycans (SNFG) is a system for symbolic representation of glycans. The system assigns unique graphical symbols for monosaccharides, and provides a much simpler representation for complex glycans, depicting both the monosaccharide building blocks and their linkages within the glycan structure. The system is widely accepted in the literature and adopted by many major journals. Updated information about the SNFG system is available here.
