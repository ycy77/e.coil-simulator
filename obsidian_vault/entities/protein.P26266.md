## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Part of the ferric enterobactin transport system. The fepE locus was originally defined by an enterobactin transport lesion; a fepE::mini-kan-9 strain is able to produce enterobactin but is unable to grow in either iron-depleted medium or with addition of purified enterobactin . A fepE homolog in TAX-90371 is responsible for very long O antigen polymerization in lipopolysaccharide, providing minor complement resistance and significant serum resistance in mouse infection models , and is upregulated during swarming . fepE is regulated by the FlhDC flagellar transcriptional regulator .
