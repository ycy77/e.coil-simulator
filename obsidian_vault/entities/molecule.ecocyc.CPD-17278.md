## Enriched Summary

CPD-12653 Stearidonate (SDA) is an ω-3 fatty acid, sometimes called moroctic acid. In mammals it is biosynthesized (in its CPD-14392 form) from LINOLENOYL-COA by the enzyme EC-1.14.19.3. CPD-12653 Stearidonate is found in the seed oils of several plants, including hemp, blackcurrant, corn gromwel and echium, where it is produced from CPD-8117 by EC-1.14.19.25. CPD-12653 Stearidonate is also produced by Group 4 cyanobacteria (such as TAX-1148), where it is esterified at the sn-1 position of glyecrolipids by EC-1.14.19.36. Like other fatty acids, CPD-12653 is rarely found in its free form. It is usually found either as CPD-14392 or CPD-17278 "incorporated into a lipid".
