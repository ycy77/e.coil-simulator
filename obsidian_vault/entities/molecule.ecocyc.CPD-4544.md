## Enriched Summary

Tellurite is the most abundant species of tellurium, a chalcogenic element related to selenium. Like other oxyanion derivatives of tellurium and selenium tellurite is toxic to living organisms even at very low levels . Bacteria have developed mechanisms to overcome its toxicity by either reducing it to elemental tellurium, which is insoluble in water and almost non-toxic , or by methylating it .
