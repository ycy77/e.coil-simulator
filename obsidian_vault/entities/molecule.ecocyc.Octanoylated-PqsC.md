## Enriched Summary

PqsC is a member of the FabH family of enzymes. The enzyme acquires an octanoyl group from CPD-196, and attaches it to a cysteine residue (C129). Together with PqsB, the proteins catalyze the coupling of CPD-19114 with the octanoate group, resulting in decarboxylation and the closing of the quinoline ring . Both PqsB and PqsC are required for the reaction. The proteins form a tight complex. Attempts to produce PqsC from TAX-287 individually in TAX-562 failed, but coexpression of both genes yielded active proteins. When copurified His-tagged PqsB and PqsC are analyzed on a native polyacrylamide gel, they produce one band even though their molecular weights differ .
