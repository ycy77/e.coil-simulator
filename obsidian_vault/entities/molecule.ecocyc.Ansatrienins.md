## Enriched Summary

The redox pair CPD-21971/CPD-21972 were discovered in 1981 from TAX-68264 under the names mycotrienin I and mycotrienin II . The same compounds were reported one year later from TAX-42684 under their currently used names . The unique structures of this family of compounds are characterized by three conjugated double bonds in the 21-membered macrolactam ring, and a cyclohexanoyl-alanyl side chain attached to the C-11 hydroxyl group of the ansa ring . They exhibit potent activity against fungi and yeasts, as well as cytotoxicity and limited antibacterial activity.
