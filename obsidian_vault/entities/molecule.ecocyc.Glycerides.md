## Enriched Summary

The name glyceride describes any ester of GLYCEROL with Fatty-Acids "fatty acids". Depending on the number of fatty acids, glycerides are classified as Monoacylglycerols monoglycerides, Diacylglycerides diglycerides and Triacylglycerols triglycerides. If a phosphate group is ester-linked to the terminal carbon of the GLYCEROL backbone, the compound is classified as Phosphoglycerides.
