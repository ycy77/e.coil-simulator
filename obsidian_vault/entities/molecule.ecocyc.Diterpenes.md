Diterpenes Diterpenoids (also referred to as diterpenes) are widely distributed in plants, marine invertebrates, and microorganisms. They are produced from the common isoprene precursor GERANYLGERANYL-PP. The diverse carbon skeletons of diterpenes are formed through carbocation-mediated cyclization cascades catalyzed by diterpene cyclases. Diterpene cyclases are categorized into two classes, class I and class II, on the basis of their reaction mechanisms and catalytic domains.
