## Enriched Summary

This compound represents an internal segment of a glycan chain within a mature peptidoglycan polymer (meso-DAP containing). It is shown with free (ie. non-cross-linked) tetrapeptide stems. The R groups represent glycan chains of β1,4-linked N-acetylglucosamine (GlcNAc) and N-acetylmuramic acid (MurNAc) residues with peptide stems of varying length (penta-, tetra- and tripeptide) - some of which may be involved in cross-links with other glycan strands. This compound has been created for use in the reaction catalysed by peptidoglycan lytic endo-transglycosylases.
