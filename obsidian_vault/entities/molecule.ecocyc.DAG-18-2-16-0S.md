This class of diacylglycerols contains two acyl chains formed by the esterification of glycerol with two fatty acids. One acyl chain has 18 carbons and two double bonds. The other acyl chain has 16 carbons and zero double bonds. In all members of the class the R group not associated with an acyl chain represents a hydrogen.
