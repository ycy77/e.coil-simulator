## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Displays esterase activity toward palmitoyl-CoA, malonyl-CoA and pNP-butyrate. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15808744}. Thioesterase activity of YbfF with large substrates such as palmitoyl-CoA and malonyl-CoA was discovered in a high-throughput screen of purified proteins . The enzyme also has general esterase activity with triacylglycerol substrates with short fatty acid chain lengths (C4, C8, C10), but not long chain lengths such as C18 . Crystal structures of the enzyme have been solved, revealing a canonical α/β hydrolase domain and a helical bundle cap domain. The potential active site catalytic tetrad was identified. Note that the crystallized protein contained four point mutations resulting in amino acid changes .
