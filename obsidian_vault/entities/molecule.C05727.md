## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: S-Substituted N-acetyl-L-cysteine; Mercapturic acid; N-Acetyl-L-cysteine S-conjugate EcoCyc common name: a mercapturate. Mercapturic acids are S-conjugates of CPD-9175, which are formed in the liver and excreted in the urine. In the process of generating a mercapturic acid, a compound is first conjugated with glutathione. This is followed by loss of the glutamate and glycine portions, followed by acetylation of the L-cysteine nitrogen to form the mercapturic acid.
