## Enriched Summary

In this reaction the transcriptional regulatory protein UhpA is phosphorylated by phospho-UhpB protein, thereby becoming activated. The site of phosphorylation on the UhpA protein is believed to be the aspartate residue Asp54. After sensor kinase-phosphotransferase UhpB autophosphorylates, the phosphoryl group is transferred to the transcriptional regulatory protein UhpA, causing its activation. In its activated state phospho-UhpA functions as the required positive activator of uhpT transcription. The UhpT protein is a transporter of hexose phosphates. EcoCyc reaction equation: UHPA-MONOMER + PHOSPHO-UHPB -> CPLX0-7754 + UHPB-MONOMER; direction=LEFT-TO-RIGHT.
