## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized protein YjfN yjfN encodes a predicted small, periplasmic protein; YjfN is a simultaneous substrate and activator of DegP proteolysis. Purified YjfN is degraded by DegP in vitro; YjfN binds to DegP and activates cleavage of a model reporter substrate; YjfN promotes the DegP-catalysed degradation of denatured OmpA and SurA-bound OmpA in vitro . Over-expression of yjfN suppresses the high temperature misfolded protein stress of cells producing a less active DegP variant (DegPA184S) . YjfN is a member of the E. coli YhcN family consisting of nine paralogous small (≤ 150 aa) proteins .
