yqaA (gene.b2689) is a gene entity. It encodes yqaA (protein.P0ADR0). Encoded protein function: Inner membrane protein YqaA EcoCyc product frame: G7407-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 2816512-2816940. EcoCyc protein note: YqaA is predicted to be an inner membrane protein with four transmembrane domains; topology analysis suggests the C-terminus is located in the cytoplasm . YqaA ia a member of the DedA family of proteins . E. coli K-12 contains 7 other DedA family members, namely EG10216 "DedA", EG11824 "YghB", EG11571 "YabI", EG12017 "YohD", G7609-MONOMER "YqjA", G6945-MONOMER "YdjX" and G6947 "YdjZ". Collectively, the DedA family is essential in E. coli K-12 . A yqaA mutant has a defect in swarming, but not swimming motility .
