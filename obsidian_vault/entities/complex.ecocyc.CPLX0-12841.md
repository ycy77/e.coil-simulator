## Enriched Summary

NrfB is a pentaheme cytochrome c protein that is believed to be the direct electron donor for cytochrome c552 (NrfA) in the formate-dependent reduction of nitrite to ammonia . NrfB purifies as a homodimer; in vitro reduced NrfB dimers serve as direct electron donors to oxidised NrfA dimers .
