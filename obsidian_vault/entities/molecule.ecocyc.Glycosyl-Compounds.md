## Enriched Summary

A glycosyl compound is a carbohydrate derivative arising formally from the elimination of water from a glycosidic hydroxy group and the attachment of the exposed carbon atom to an oxygen, carbon, nitrogen or sulfur atom of a second entity from which an H atom has been removed.
