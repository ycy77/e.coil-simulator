## Enriched Summary

Several methylated or demethylated forms of menaquinone have been identified, including 8-methylmenaquinone, which is found in a diverse range of microorganisms such as members of the bacterial phyla TAX-201174 Actinobacteria and TAX-1224 Proteobacteria as well as the archaeal phyla TAX-28889 Crenarchaeota and TAX-28890 Euryarchaeota . The addition of methyl groups to menaquinone shifts the redox potential to more negative values , making these derivatives good redox mediators in electron transport chains that use low-potential electron acceptors such as polysulfide or sulfite .
