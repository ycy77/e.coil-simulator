## Enriched Summary

2-3-Butanediols 2,3-butanediol is a bacterial/fungal fermentation product of commercial interest, mostly as an antifreeze agent due to to its low freezing point . One of its well known applications is the formation of methyl ethyl ketone, by dehydration, which can be used as a liquid fuel additive . All three stereo isomer of 2,3-butanediol (BUTANEDIOL, CPD-346 and CPD-12004) can be produced by various organisms . The metabolic function of 2,3-butanediol is not yet known, although it may play a role in preventing intracellular acidification by changing the metabolism from acid production to the formation of a neutral compound .
