## Enriched Summary

CPD-7975 "2-Heptyl-4-hydroxyquinoline N-oxide" (HQNO) is a respiratory toxin produced by TAX-287 that suppresses the growth of many Gram-positive bacteria . HQNO is potent inhibitor of respiratory electron transfer . It binds to the quinone reduction site of the cytochrome bc1 complex and inhibits quinol oxidase activity of cytochrome bo3, bd, and type II NADH-quinone oxidoreductase .
