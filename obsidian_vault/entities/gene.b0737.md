## Enriched Summary

tolQ (gene.b0737) is a gene entity. It encodes tolQ (protein.P0ABU9). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: Part of the Tol-Pal system, which plays a role in outer membrane invagination during cell division and is important for maintaining outer membrane integrity (PubMed:1683466, PubMed:17233825). Required, with TolR, for the proton motive force-dependent activation of TolA and for TolA-Pal interaction (PubMed:11722743). The Tol-Pal system is also required for polar localization of chemoreceptors clusters (PubMed:24720726). The system also appears to be required for the activity of several outer membrane-localized enzymes with cell wall remodeling activity (PubMed:32152098).
