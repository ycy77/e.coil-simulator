## Enriched Summary

This compound represents a membrane attached, nascent peptidoglycan polymer (meso-diaminopimelate containing). It contains an L,D cross link formed between the L-centre of meso-DAP of a peptide on one glycan chain to the D-centre of meso-DAP from a separate glycan chain. The R groups represent glycan chains of β1,4-linked N-acetylglucosamine and N-acetylmuramic acid residues with peptide stems of varying length (penta-, tetra- and tripeptide) - some of which may be involved in cross-links with other glycan strands.
