## Enriched Summary

yqiI (gene.b3048) is a gene entity. It encodes yqiI (protein.P76656). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: May be involved in a fimbrial system chaperoned by YqiH and exported by YqiG. EcoCyc product frame: G7587-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 3190632-3191696. EcoCyc protein note: YqiI may play a role in detoxification of methylglyoxal (MG) and dihydroxyacetone (DHA). A yqiI insertion mutant increases the sensitivity of a G6891 gloA mutant to MG and DHA; a strain containing only the yqiI insertion mutation does not show increased sensitivity to MG or DHA . The yqiI insertion mutant appears to lack flagellin and shows impaired motility on swarm plates . E. coli K-12 contains 12 chaperone-usher fimbrial operons, including ygiLyqiGHI; a fimbria-lacking mutant - strain WQM026 - created by deleting all 12 operons has been characterised .
