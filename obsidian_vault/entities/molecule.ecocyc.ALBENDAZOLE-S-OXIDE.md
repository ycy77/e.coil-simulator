## Enriched Summary

ALBENDAZOLE Albendazole and CPD-20524 are benzimidazole compounds used as broad-spectrum anthelmintics against gastrointestinal nematodes and the larval stages of cestodes . Both compounds are oxidized by EC-1.14.14.73, and EC-1.14.13.32, to an S-oxide, which is the form responsible for their systemic biological activity .
