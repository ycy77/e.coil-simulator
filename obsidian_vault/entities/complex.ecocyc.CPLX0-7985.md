## Enriched Summary

MscM (fomerly YjeP) is a mechanosensitive ion channel of miniconductance in E. coli K-12. MscM gates spontaneously in response to increased membrane tension and overexpression of mscM in a cell line lacking all known mechanosensitive channels protects cells against hypoosmotic shock . MscM is a member of the small mechanosensitive ion channel (MscS) family . mscM deletion mutants exhibit no detectable phenotype . Expression of mscM increased after kasugamycin challenge of a relA strain . MscM is likely to form homoheptameric channels . McsM is predicted to have 11 transmembrane spans and an extensive periplasmic domain . Seven mechanosensitive ion channels have been characterised in E. coli K-12: CPLX0-7627 "MscL", EG11160-MONOMER "MscS", G6255-MONOMER "MscK", MscM, CPLX0-7982 "YnaI", CPLX0-7983 "YbiO" and CPLX0-7981 "YbdG".
