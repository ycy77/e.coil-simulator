## Enriched Summary

Luciferase-like monooxygenase yhbW encodes a predicted luciferase-like monooxygenase . YhbW abundance is more than 77-fold increased during growth with vanillin, but the purified enzyme does not appear to be able to oxidize vanillin to vanillic acid . yhbW is a multicopy suppressor of the lethal phenotype of inhibition of σE. In the presence of overexpressed yhbW, σE is no longer required for viability .
