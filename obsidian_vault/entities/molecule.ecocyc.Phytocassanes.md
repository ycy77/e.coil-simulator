## Enriched Summary

The phytocassanes are rice phytoalexins that were isolated from TAX-148305 infected rice leaves or TAX-456999 infected rice stems. The first four were discovered in 1995 and named phytocassanes A-D , and were followed by a fifth member (phytocassane E) in 1997 . It should be noted that several years later it was shown that the rice phytocassanes are configured with ent-, rather than the originally suggested normal stereochemistry and, thus, are enantiomeric to the originally suggested chemical structures .
