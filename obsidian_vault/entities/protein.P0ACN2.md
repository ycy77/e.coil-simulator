Uncharacterized HTH-type transcriptional regulator YtfH YtfH, which contains a helix-turn-helix motif to bind DNA in its N-terminal domain, appears to belong to the RipR family of transcription factors, and it was predicted to regulate genes related to metabolism . YtfH was mentioned as a predicted inner membrane protein that may be a substrate for the periplasmic chaperone YfgM .
