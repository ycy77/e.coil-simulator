## Enriched Summary

Glucuronan is a polysaccharide composed of CPD-12521 residues connected by 1→4 linkages. The glucuronan produced by the bacterium TAX-382 is variably O-acetylated at C-3 and/or C-2 positions depending on the Mg2+ concentration in the culture medium.
