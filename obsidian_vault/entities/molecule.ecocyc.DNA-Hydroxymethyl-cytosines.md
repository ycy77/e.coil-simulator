## Enriched Summary

This compound class stands for generic and unspecified DNA 5-hydroxymethylcytosines. Phage T4 DNA differs from typical DNA molecules in that it contains no CYTOSINE. Instead, in contains the modified base CPD-15531 which pairs with GUANINE. The hydroxy group is often glucosylated, as in Alpha-Glycosylated-Hydroxymethyl-DNAs "α-glucosyl-hydroxymethylcytosine", Beta-Glycosylated-Hydroxymethylated-DNAs "β-glucosyl-hydroxymethylcytosine" and Gluc-Glycosyl-Hydroxymethylcytosine-DNAs "β-glucosyl-(1→4)-β-glucosyl-hydroxymethylcytosine", so that T4 DNA is typically covered with sugar chains.
