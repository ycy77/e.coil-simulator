## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized serine protease YdgD (EC 3.4.21.-) YdgD is a predicted periplasmic serine protease that may be involved in cell envelope protein quality control . Sequence similarity suggests that it may contain β-barrel structure(s) . A ydgD single mutant does not have a phenotype under the tested conditions; however, a EG10760 tsp ydgD double mutant does not grow in the presence of SDS/EDTA at either 37 or 42°C and shows increased membrane permeability. Expression of rpoE is induced 2.3-fold, and expression of spy is induced 4.3-fold in the tsp ydgD double mutant .
