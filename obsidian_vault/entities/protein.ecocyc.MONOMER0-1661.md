## Enriched Summary

In most cells which have been analyzed, folic acid cofactors have been reported to exist mainly as polyglutamate derivatives. Since the late 1940s it has been assumed that the glutamates are linked in amide bonds via the γ-COOH groups. In 1986 Ferone et al have shown that in E. coli, while the first three glutamates are linked by their γ-COOH groups, additional glutamate residues are linked via α-COOH groups. In a later work Ferone et al have shown that extracts of E. coli contained an enzymatic activity which catalyzed the addition of L-glutamate to the α-carboxyl of various polyglutamate substrates, including folylpolyglutamates.
