## Enriched Summary

The crystal structure of EutS has been determined at 1.65 Å resolution. The α/β structure resembles that of carboxysomal shell proteins, although the cyclic hexameric structure has a bend of approximately 40°. This may allow formation of the edges of a polyhedral microcompartment shell . A G39V mutant protein forms a flat, symmetric hexameric structure . The functions of the orthologous eut operon genes for ethanolamine utilization in Salmonella enterica have been studied experimentally .
