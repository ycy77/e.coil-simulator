## Enriched Summary

Clindamycin is a lincosamide antibiotic used in clinical practice. It is a derivatives of CPD-13893 and is prepared semisynthetically. Clindamycin exerts its bacteriostatic function by inhibiting the peptidyltransferase reaction on the 50S ribosomal subunit and blocking protein synthesis in sensitive bacterial strains .
