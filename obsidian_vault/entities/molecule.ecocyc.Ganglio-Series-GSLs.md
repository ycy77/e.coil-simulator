The lipid portion is embedded in the outer plasma membrane leaflet and the sugar chains extended to the outer environment, acting as an antigen. Together with Glycoproteins glycoproteins and Glycosaminoglycans glycosaminoglycans, the glycosphingolipids contribute to the glycocalyx that covers eukaryotic cell surfaces.
