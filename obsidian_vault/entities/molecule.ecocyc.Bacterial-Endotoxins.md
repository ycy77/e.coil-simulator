## Enriched Summary

This compound class stands for generic and unspecified bacterial endotoxins. These are components from the outer membrane and cell wall of bacteria, which are toxic to humans and can cause sepsis. The Gram negative bacteria produce lipopolysaccharides, whereas the Gram positive bacteria produce the less active lipoteichoic acids and muramyl peptides.
