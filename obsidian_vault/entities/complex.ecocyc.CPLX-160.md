## Enriched Summary

FrwCBDPtsA is a putative PTS permease which belongs to the functional superfamily of the phosphoenolpyruvate (PEP)-dependent, sugar transporting phosphotransferase system (PTSsugar). The PTSsugar transports and simultaneously phosphorylates its sugar substrates in a process called group translocation (reviewed by ). FrwC, FrwB, FrwD and PtsA (formerly FrwA) are all encoded within the frw gene cluster. The Frw proteins and protein domains are homologous to constituents of the fructose Enzyme II complexes. The frw gene cluster also encodes several enzymes concerned with anaerobic carbon metabolism . FrwCBDPtsA is a member of the PTS Fructose-Mannitol (Fru) family of transporters .
