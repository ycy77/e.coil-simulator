## Enriched Summary

2,6-dichlorophenolindophenol ( DCPIP ) is a compound that is often used as a redox dye. DCPIP "Oxidized DCPIP" is blue, DCPIPH, which is reduced by a single electron, is pink, and fully CPD-13079 "reduced DCPIP" is colorless. It is often used in measurements of the electron transport chain in plants because of its higher affinity for electrons than ferredoxin.
