## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Galactitol; Dulcitol; Dulcose Galactitol (dulcitol) is an acyclic sugar alcohol with a widespread distribution among plant life . It is found, for example, in seaweed and is the sole constituent of the sugar-rich sticky liquid, secreted by planthoppers of the TAX-30089 family, also known as honeydew or Madagascar Manna. Glactitol also occurs in the cataractous lenses of galactosemic animals as a metabolic product of galactose, causing a hypertonic condition whereby water is drawn into the lens fibres, which become opaque and may eventually rupture .
