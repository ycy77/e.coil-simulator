## Enriched Summary

Putative outer membrane porin protein NmpC NmpC is a member of the General Bacterial Porin (GBP) Family . It is believed to be a water filled outer membrane porin, with an effective diameter of 1.2nm . It is a silent gene within E. coli K-12 . Mutational studies which activate the gene show that the porin associates itself with the peptidoglycan . Expression of these porins increases sensitivity to several colicins and phages . However, whether this sensitivity is specific or not, is unknown. nmpC: new membrane porin C .
