## Enriched Summary

The two enantiomers of Limonenes "limonene" are the most-abundant monocyclic monoterpenes in nature. CPD-4886 (L-limonene) is found in a variety of trees and herbs such as Mentha spp., while CPD-8785 (D-limonene) is the major component of peel oil from oranges and lemons, and the essential oil of caraway. The natural functions of limonene are thought to include prevention of dehydration and inhibition of microbial (especially fungal) growth .
