## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Probably a ferredoxin oxidoreductase that catalyzes the oxidation of aldehydes. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:30945846}. YdhV contains a redox-active CPD-15874 bis-Mo-MPT cofactor. Bis-Mo-MPT, which lacks the linked GMP moieties of CPD-15873, has not previously been observed as the cofactor of a molybdoenzyme . YdhV is a putative member of the aldehyde ferredoxin oxidoreductase family of molybdoenzymes . Based on sequence similarity, the ydhYVWXUT operon may encode components of an oxidoreductase . A ydhY-T mutant does not show a growth defect under anaerobic conditions, although utilization of certain sources of sulfur is impaired . In a ΔtusA strain, expression of ydhV is decreased in mid-exponential phase and under aerobic conditions . Reviews:
