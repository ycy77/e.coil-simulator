## Enriched Summary

CPD-19461 "Cyclobis-(1→6)-α-nigerosyl" is a cyclic glucotetraose with alternate α(1→6)- and α(1→3)-glucosidic linkages, produced by several bacterial species during the degradation of complex glycans. The compound was first described from TAX-1245, in which it was formed by the hydrolysis of Alternans alternan . It was later reported that in TAX-1459 it is synthesized from MALTOTETRAOSE . Similar enzymes were reported from a Bacillus sp. and an TAX-1665 strain . Following its import into the cell, the compound is degraded, serving as a carbon source .
