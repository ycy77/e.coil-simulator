## Enriched Summary

L-Iduronate is a reducing acidic sugar that can form a ring structure, as shown for CPD-12515. CPD-12515 is the major uronic acid component of the glycosaminoglycans Dermatan-Sulfate and HEPARIN. It is also present in Heparan-Sulfate, but in a minor amount relative to its carbon-5 epimer GLUCURONATE. CPD-12515 is often modified by the addition of an O-sulfate group at carbon position 2 to form CPD-12518. The open-chain structure shown here is provided only as a schematic illustration of chirality.
