## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Part of a cryptic operon that is poorly expressed in vivo. May be an ancestral sugar porin with a broad carbohydrate specificity; it binds aromatic beta-D-glucosides such as arbutin and salicin, but with low affinity compared to the binding of maltooligosaccharides to the LamB porin. BglH is an outer-membrane porin coded for by a gene in the cryptic bgl operon. When expressed, it forms a slightly voltage-dependent membrane channel that weakly binds carbohydrates. Its greatest binding affinity is for β-glucosides .
