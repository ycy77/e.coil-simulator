## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: N-Acylsphingosine; Ceramide; N-Acylsphing-4-enine; (4E)-Sphing-4-enine ceramide EcoCyc common name: an N-acylsphingosine. A N-Acylsphingosine "sphingosine ceramide" is Ceramides molecule that is composed of SPHINGOSINE and Fatty-Acids. This type of ceramide is common in animals, and is produced by desaturation of a sphinganine base within a ceramide (known as CPD3DJ-82) by EC-1.14.19.17. Ceramides are found in high concentrations within the cell membrane and are one of the component lipids that make up Sphingomyelins sphingomyelin, one of the major lipids in the lipid bilayer.
