## Enriched Summary

CPD-18578 Phenolphthiocerol-based glycolipids have been found in TAX-1769, TAX-1768, TAX-1765, TAX-1781, and TAX-78331 . The phenolic glycolipid CPD-18679 is the major PGL of TAX-1765, and is found in small amounts in hypervirulent strains of TAX-1773 that produce CPD-18579. The phenolphthiocerol backbone in this compound is attached to the monosaccharide (2-O-methyl-α-L-rhamnoside) and the two secondary alcohol functions are esterified by Mycocerosic-Acids "mycocerosic acids" .
