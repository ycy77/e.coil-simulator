## Enriched Summary

The jadomycins are a unique family of angucycline-derived antibiotics . Jadomycin B is produced by the organism TAX-953739 under stress conditions such as heat shock, phage infection, and particularly ethanol treatment . This compound is bioactive against Gram-positive (including the methicillin-resistant TAX-1280) and Gram-negative bacteria, and against yeast . Its structure includes the deoxysugar CPD-11914 . Jadomycin B exists as a diastereomeric mixture of CPD-12934 and CPD-21697 , with the former being the predominant form at a ratio of 3:2. The two forms can interconvert via an aldehyde intermediate .
