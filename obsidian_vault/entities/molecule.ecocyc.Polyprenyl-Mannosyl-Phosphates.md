## Enriched Summary

Polyprenol monophospho-β-D-mannose, or PPM, is the bacterial equivalents of the eukaryotic CPD-171 "dolichol monophospho-β-D-mannose", which is used as mannose donor in various eukaryotic glycosylation processes. PPMs are found in Mycobacteria, where they serve as mannose donors for the biosynthesis of lipomannan and lipoarabinomanan, key bacterial factors termed modulins.
