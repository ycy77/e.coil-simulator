## Enriched Summary

YfcF has glutathione S-transferase activity with 1-chloro-2,4-dinitrobenzene and GSH-dependent peroxidase activity toward cumene hydroperoxide . Site-directed mutagenesis of the predicted active site residue Ser16 showed that it is important for catalytic activity . YfcF is homodimeric in the crystal structure (unpublished). Deletion of yfcF decreases the resistance to hydrogen peroxide . yfcF shows decreased expression in biofilms of an rpoS mutant .
