## Enriched Summary

Fosmidomycin, an antibiotic isolated from TAX-1914, is a potent inhibitor of EC-1.1.1.267, a key enzyme of the NONMEVIPP-PWY "methylerythritol phosphate pathway", an alternative pathway of isoprenoid synthesis. Many Eubacteria, as well as the malaria parasite TAX-5820, are dependent on this pathway, because they lack the mevalonate-based pathway.
