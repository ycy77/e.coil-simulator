## Enriched Summary

Phytoalexins are antimicrobial substances synthesized de novo by plants that accumulate rapidly at areas of incompatible pathogen infection. Phytoalexins do not form a coherent chemical class - they are chemically diverse, with different types characteristic of particular plant species.
