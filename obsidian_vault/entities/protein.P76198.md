Inner membrane transport protein YdiN In the Transporter Classification Database, YdiN is an uncharacterised member of the Aromatic Acid:H+ Symporter (AAHS) Family within the Major Facilitator Superfamily (MFS) . YdiN is predicted to be an inner membrane protein with twelve transmembrane domains; topology analysis suggests the C-terminus is located in the cytoplasm . Expression of ydiN is induced under carbon-limited conditions in a shikimate-producing strain .
