## Enriched Summary

The rifamycins are a group of antibiotics that are particularly effective against mycobacteria. They are used to treat tuberculosis, leprosy, and mycobacterium avium complex (MAC) infections. The rifamycins include both natural products such as RIFAMYCIN-B and synthetic derivatives such as CPD-18727.
