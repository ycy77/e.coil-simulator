## Enriched Summary

Corphins are hexahydroporphinoid ligands that combine structural elements of both the Porphyrins porphyrins and the Corrins corrins. They contain the carbon-nitrogen skeleton of porphyrins and a chromophore system similar to that occurring in corrins. Pyrrocorphins are corphin tautomers in which one of the four five-membered rings is in pyrrolic form .
