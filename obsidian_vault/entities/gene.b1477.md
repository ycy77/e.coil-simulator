YddM was confirmed as a TF through an integrated workflow comprised of computational prediction, knowledge-based classification, and experimental validation of candidate TFs at the genome scale . Compared to known global TFs, YddM exhibits some interesting regulatory features. First, it has more intragenic binding peaks and fewer peaks located within putative regulatory regions.
