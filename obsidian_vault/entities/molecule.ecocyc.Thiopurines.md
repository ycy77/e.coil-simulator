## Enriched Summary

The thiopurine drugs CPD-20732, CPD-15916 and CPD-5721 are purine antimetabolites that are widely used in the treatment of acute lymphoblastic leukemia, autoimmune disorders (e.g., Crohn's disease, rheumatoid arthritis), and organ transplant recipients. The drugs are eventually inactivated by the action of EC-2.1.1.67 (TPMT), which catalyzes their S-methylation.
