## Enriched Summary

Phenylalanine--tRNA ligase operon leader peptide (pheST attenuator peptide) The PheM leader peptide controls by attenuation the expression of the pheMST operon (which codes for phenylalanyl-tRNA synthetase) in response to phenylalanine availability . Specifically, decreased levels of charged tRNAPhe lead to derepression of pheMST . Although other ORFs have been detected upstream from pheS, PheM appears to be the only functional one .
