## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized protein YejG Overexpression of YejG leads to slightly increased resistance to the aminoglycoside antibiotics apramycin, sisomicin and tobramycin . A solution structure of YejG shows that the protein has a stucture similar to the RNA recognition motif domain III of EG10360-MONOMER. However, YejG does not appear to co-purify with the ribosome or bind to nucleic acids . Expression of yejG is slightly upregulated in response to treatment with citral, and a ΔyejG mutant is inhibited more strongly by citral .
