Uncharacterized protein YceK Strong aggravating genetic interactions (measured by relative colony size and fitness in rich medium) were observed between lptD and yceK . Deletion of yceK results in impaired outer membrane integrity as evidenced by vancomycin-induced morphological defects and lowered outer membrane protein (OMP) abundance .
