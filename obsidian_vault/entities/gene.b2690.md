## Enriched Summary

yqaB (gene.b2690) is a gene entity. It encodes yqaB (protein.P77475). Encoded protein function: FUNCTION: Catalyzes strongly the dephosphorylation of fructose-1-phosphate (Fru1P) and slightly the dephosphorylation of 6-phosphogluconate (6P-Glu). It has low beta-phosphoglucomutase activity. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15808744, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16990279}. EcoCyc product frame: G7408-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 2816937-2817503. EcoCyc protein note: YqaB is a sugar phosphatase belonging to the superfamily of haloacid dehalogenase (HAD)-like hydrolases. In addition to its main fructose-1-phosphatase activity, YqaB has low β-phosphoglucomutase activity . The phosphatase activity of YqaB was first discovered in a high-throughput screen of purified proteins . No measurable sorbitol-6-phosphate phosphatase activity was detected .
