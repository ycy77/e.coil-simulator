## Enriched Summary

The ripe cones of the hop plant (TAX-3486) are covered with glandular hairs containing a mixture of essential oil and bitter acids. These compounds are important not only for the beer brewing industry but also for their antimicrobial, antifungal and antifeedant activities . The bitter acids consist of the so-called α- and β-acids. The former group is of particular importance for the brewing industry as they are transformed into iso-α-acids such as CPD-7116 and CPD-7115 during the brewing process, conferring the bitter taste of beer .
