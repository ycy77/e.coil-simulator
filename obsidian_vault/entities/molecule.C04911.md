Ganglioside are components of the cell plasma membrane that modulates cell signal transduction events, and appear to concentrate in lipid rafts. The name ganglioside is derived from ganglion cells of the brain, from which the first gangliosides were isolated by the German scientist Ernst Klenk in 1942.
