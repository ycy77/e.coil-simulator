## Enriched Summary

CPD-16768 Mycosamine (3-amino, 3-6 dideoxy D-mannose) is the amino sugar present in polyene macrolide antibiotics, represented by Candicidins, CPD-17211, CPD-17212 and CPD-17213. The amino sugar component mycosamine plays a critical role in conferring the antifungal properties exhibited by polyene antibiotics . Mycosamine is synthesized in a CPD-17206 "GDP-activated form" from the primary metabolite GDP-MANNOSE.
