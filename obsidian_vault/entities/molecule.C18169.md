## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Diacylglyceryl-N,N,N-trimethylhomoserine; DGTS EcoCyc common name: a diacylglyceryl-N,N,N-trimethyl-L-homoserine. Betaine lipids are non-phosphorous glycerolipid analogs of phosphatidylcholine. DGTS is a betaine lipid studied in phosphate starved cells of the purple bacterium TAX-1063. It was first discovered in the unicellular alga TAX-2986 , and has also been found in TAX-3055, other lower plants and fungi. It has been proposed to take the place of phosphatidylcholine in the membranes of these organisms. DGTS is a constituent of a fraction of thylakoid membranes and contains predominantly hexadecanoic and octadecanoic fatty acids .
