## Enriched Summary

CPD-9247 "cis-Vaccenate" is produced from CPD-9245 by bacteria and plants. It is one of only two unsaturated fatty acids in TAX-562 (see PWY-5973). Some plant species such as TAX-48545 and TAX-73871 (Doxantha unguis-cati L.) produce relatively high levels of CPD-9245 and CPD-9247 in their seed oils . The plant pathway is likely identical to the bacterial one (in ). Like other fatty acids, CPD-9247 is rarely found in its free form.
