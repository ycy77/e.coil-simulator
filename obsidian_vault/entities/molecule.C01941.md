## Enriched Summary

KEGG compound: Nocardicin A The antibiotic NOCARDICIN-A is a member of the monocyclic, monolactam class of β-lactam antibiotics and is resistant to β-lactamase enzymes . It is produced by the actinomycetes TAX-53432 and TAX-40567 (it has been suggested that the former should be reclassified under TAX-40566) and shows moderate antibiotic activity against a broad spectrum of Gram-negative bacteria . Organisms that produce NOCARDICIN-A also produce other nocardicins (CPD-19210, CPD-9415, CPD-19211, NOCARDICIN-E, CPD-9417 and CPD-9418), although nocardicin A is the major metabolite and the most active.
