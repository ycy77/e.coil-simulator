## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Regulatory protein for the formate hydrogenlyase system. Could act by directly interacting with FhlA or by preventing the binding of FhlA to the upstream activatory sequence (PubMed:1625581). Also down-regulates expression of the hyf operon (PubMed:12426353). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12426353, ECO:0000269|PubMed:1625581}. This regulator participates in controlling several genes involved in the formate hydrogenlyase system . The mechanism of regulation by HycA is unknown. HycA may interact directly with the FhlA protein or prevent the binding of FhlA to activator sequences. Expression of hycA is activated by formate and hypophosphite (a formate analog) .
