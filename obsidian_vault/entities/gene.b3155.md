## Enriched Summary

yhbQ (gene.b3155) is a gene entity. It encodes yhbQ (protein.P45472). Encoded protein function: UPF0213 protein YhbQ EcoCyc product frame: G7649-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 3299966-3300268. EcoCyc protein note: Purified YhbQ has metal dependent nuclease activity; genetic interaction screening suggests that YhbQ functions during DNA damage repair . Purified YhbQ has metal dependent 3' to 5' exonuclease activity on ssDNA; purified YhbQ has metal dependent endonuclease activity on circular ssDNA and linear dsDNA but not on circular dsDNA or RNA; the divalent cations Mg2+, Mn2+ and Co2+ all support enzyme activity . Purified YhbQ is active on several substrates related to nucleotide excision repair (NER), including dsDNA substrates containing a single nucleotide mismatch or ssDNA loops, and on DNA recombination intermediates . A strain lacking yhbQ shows hypersensitivity to UV irradiation and to the DNA damaging agents mitomycin C and 4-nitroquinoline-1-oxide . Affinity-tagged YhbQ co-purifies with PD03831 "DnaA", EG10823-MONOMER "RecA", G7656-MONOMER "ObgE" and EG11062-MONOMER "UvrB" .
