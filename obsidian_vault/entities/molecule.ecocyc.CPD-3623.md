## Enriched Summary

CPD-3623 Sebacate (sebacic acid) is a naturally occurring dicarboxylic acid. It is found in the sebum (the secretion of sebaceous glands) and other lipids that coat the skin surface. Sebacate is used commercially as a monomer for nylon 610 and in the production of plasticizers, lubricants, hydraulic fluids, cosmetics, candles, and other products.
