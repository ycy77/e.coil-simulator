## Enriched Summary

Alpha-NAD-P is a class of compounds including the instances CPD-16008 and CPD-16007. They should not be confused with the common electron carriers known simply as NAD-P-OR-NOP, whose full name is β-NAD(P)+. The compounds differ in the stereo configuration of the bond between the ribose and the nicotinamide base, which points up in α-NAD(P)+ and down in β-NAD(P)+.
