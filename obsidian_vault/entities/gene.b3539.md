## Enriched Summary

yhjV (gene.b3539) is a gene entity. It encodes yhjV (protein.P37660). Encoded protein function: Inner membrane transport protein YhjV EcoCyc product frame: YHJV-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 3700563-3701834. EcoCyc protein note: The YhjV protein is an uncharacterised member of the Hydroxy/Aromatic Amino Acid Permease (HAAAP) Family within the Amino Acid-Polyamine-Organocation (APC) Superfamily . YhjV is implicated in melatonin transport; a yhjV knockout strain has decreased growth in the presence of melatonin (4g/L) and overexpression of yhjV improves melatonin production in a strain engineered for its synthesis . YhjV has 11 transmembrane helices and the C-terminus is located in the periplasm . A yhjV deletion mutant is 2-fold more sensitive to both X-radiation and UV-radiation than wild type .
