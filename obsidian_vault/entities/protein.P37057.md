## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Cryptic version of the phage P2 OGR protein which acts as an activator of P2 late transcription. OgrK is encoded as part of a prophage P2 remnant within the E. coli K-12 genome ; OgrK is a homolog of the P2-encoded Ogr protein that controls P2 late transcription . E. coli K-12 supports the growth of an ogr-defective P2 phage .
