## Enriched Summary

yhgF (gene.b3407) is a gene entity. It encodes yhgF (protein.P46837). Encoded protein function: Protein YhgF EcoCyc product frame: G7746-MONOMER. Genomic coordinates: 3537385-3539706. EcoCyc protein note: YhgF consists of multiple predicted domains, among them an S1 domain, commonly found in extensively researched RNA-binding proteins (RBPs) . In a large genetic interaction screen, yhgF showed genetic interactions with a number of proteins involved in translation and ribosome biogenesis. A ΔyhgF mutant showed increased levels of stop codon readthrough . CLIP-seq showed YhgF to bind 52 different RNAs and the expression of the rmf mRNA was shown to be affected by the over-expression of YhgF . Evolutive analysis showed that YhgF is present in all domains of life . yhgF insertion mutants were identified in a genetic screen for genes that are important for survival of exposure to ionizing radiation (IR). A yhgF deletion mutant had a modest decrease in IR survival .
