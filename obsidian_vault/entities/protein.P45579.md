## Enriched Summary

FUNCTION: Catalyzes the NADPH-dependent reduction of 2-oxoglutarate and 2-oxobutanoate, leading to the respective 2-hydroxycarboxylate. Cannot use NADH instead of NADPH as a redox partner. Do not catalyze the reverse reactions. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:27941785}. The NADP-dependent hydroxycarboxylate dehydrogenase activity of HcxA was discovered in a large-scale metabolomics screen for enzymatic activities of uncharacterized proteins in E. coli . An hcxA null mutant showed strongly repressed swarming motility . HcxA: "hydroxycarboxylic acid dehydrogenase A"
