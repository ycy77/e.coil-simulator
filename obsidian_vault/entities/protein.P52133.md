## Enriched Summary

Uncharacterized HTH-type transcriptional regulator YfjR YfjR, which contains a helix-turn-helix motif to bind DNA in its N-terminal domain, appears to belong to the DeoR family of transcription factors, and it was predicted to regulate genes involved in processes related with phages and with biofilm formation . The transcription of the yfjR gene is affected by conditions that lead to biofilm formation , and it increases 16-fold upon deletion of tqsA .
